dust in the wind
a message to Yama







Dearest Father Death,

Alas, a time of distrubing circumstances has come to this, our adopted home. I fear that the golden age of Troad has come to an end with the destruction caused by Chrysofar and her rose. The times of parring with sabers to draw a small wound against each other has past and now everyone and their dog is bashing with bigger mallets to take out as many as possible.

For a second there I thouht I would martyr myself to break the accord and the book of new day, but when playing out the probabilities of outcomes, I always saw that redlight witch coming in a taking my gold card while I was down, so decided against it.

I for one, as a Dust Law character am appalled by her actions. Surely there are better ways to accomplish her goals. Instead, she has decided to throw the whole chessboard away and say that there is no game anymore.

As a duely recognized representive of the church of the greylord, I am considering demanding her removal as last defender.She deserves that title a much as you would deserve one of "the happiest man on earth" award.

Some action against her must be taken, and I know that soon it will. And it wont be pretty, but for now I'm not holding my breath for change from her (haha).

As for plans for Troad, I am in the process of usurping the empire, and now believe I have the cover needed to do so.

- Kalkin
