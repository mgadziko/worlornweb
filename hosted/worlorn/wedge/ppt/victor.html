Victor's Knife

Victor, son of Dira, uncovered this little ditty on the archetype of a switchblade he discovered: 

Ripper Jack's a friend of mine
he keeps me on a tether.
when the sun is high and fine
we stay home together.
when the night is cold and grey
he takes me from his sack;
and when he lets me out to play
don't ever turn your back!

Nowdays, knives based on that archetype are said to be carried by Snake Crusaders.
