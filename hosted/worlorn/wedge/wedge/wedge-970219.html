Adventure record - February 19, 1997

?-??-???? alt.Llewghund

Shayol begins dancing, joined by Almitra, alt.Wedge and alt.Marisse. It is still winter, and redlight is still continuous. Shayol performs his dance of preparation. We set sail for Musquel; the journey takes eight days. On the beach, Josie (wearing an eyepatch) accuses Wedge of betraying Ko. Her "army" throw spears, damaging Wedge and Almitra. We reboard the ship, and see six ships of the sea cutting us off from the open sea. Some source (Josie using Musquel Turf) prevents Wedge's access to Watersea. Almitra assumes unicorn form.

One of the ships launches a meteor swarm which destroys the ship, but none of us are killed (for various reasons). We grab onto wreckage to stay afloat. We are rescued by Yama, who brings us (except Marisse) through his mirror while giving the illusion that we are being eaten by sharks. He lets Marisse actually die.

In this universe, Yama physically resembles Ko. He says that the other Turfs have probably already attacked and "toasted" Morgan in Llewghund. During the discussion, Almitra says the name "Threnody" -- we (except Yama) find ourselves on a featureless plain. In the distance we see a black tower. We walk to it. It turns out to be the towe of "Lofty Llewellyn", we are informed by the doorkeeper, Tregellish Mul. Llewellyn lets Almitra enter, and she vouches for the rest of us.

We learn that in this universe, the Compact is titled "Scroll of the East Wind".

Llewellyn summoned Almitra here to sleep with him and bear his child. She refuses for 18 days. On the 19th day, just as she had decided to change her mind, we find that Llewellyn, Tregellish Mul, and the full contents of the tower (except for us) has disappeared.

Wedge removes the party to an island in the Watersea. He perceives that the Light of the World is in the same location as winter and redlight are coming from. Apparently Llyr was slain by redlight, but now "possesses" his familiar, the great white dragon Frost. An emissary arrives from Llyr/Frost, and negotiates with Wedge. They agree to trade the water heart for the gold card Light of the World and a ship of the sea, as well as an emerald box in which to carry the gold card. Llyr/Frost provides a full emerald box, which the emissary says contains a being who can make another emerald box. When Wedge returns with an empty emerald box, the trade can be made.

We set sail for Llewghund. The journey takes 18 days. Llewghund is again a ruin, with everyone slain after a great battle. The Half-Elven One's body is found beheaded, with the head missing. Almitra sits of the Throne, but fails her save, turning into a unicorn to survive.

Wedge opens the emerald box, and Tanngrisner is released from it. Wedge commands him into service to make an emerald box as payment for rescuing him from Llyr's dungeon. Tann agrees. Tann says that his lab is at Diaspar. We trump there, which astounds Tann -- this universe has no Trump.

The dome is sealed, but Tann knows how to open it. As we enter, we breath the diazepam drug in the air which puts us all to sleep, except Shayol. Dira approaches him with a morganti weapon. Shayol invites Dira out, and Dira invites Shayol in. Apparently this city is a trap: one can only enter if invited, and can only exit if invited. This is what Dira has been waiting for, but as he attempts to leave, he jumps back in time to avoid being affected by redlight. Dira tells Shayol that he (Dira) was imprisoned here by Tanngrisner, but by his own consent.

Shayol tells Dira that Almitra will die if she resumes human form. Dira wishes "I wish all forms of this woman were here alive now", not knowing who he was wishing about -- Almitra becomes Rib, sentient Deb. She discovers that only a peer (another sentient or demonlord) can invite her out of Diaspar. She takes unicorn form, and all the gear of all Deb's characters falls out onto the floor. Dira points out Marlee's hook. Rib takes Marlee form, and uses the hook to bring Tanngrisner and Wedge back onto the adventure.

Dira turns all the Lolth galvorn wish rings into snake wish rings. We all end dreaming, and Shayol ends dancing, since Rib can begin adventuring here. As we are in Dira's favor, the diazepam will not be renewed next adventure.
