Adventure record - August 28, 1996

1-29-4489 (45 Necrodemus). diGriz, Shan, and Cory (a glass golem in white robes) trump to Wedge and Marisse in the ship at High Asgar. Cory transforms into a fire rat.

We hear sounds of fighting from on deck. diGriz opens door; there is a tanned "Adonis" with a green trident in the way who says "Take me to your leader." Wedge points to diGriz. The sounds of fighting end. The tanned guy makes a speech of gibberish. Marisse says that ten years ago, these emissaries from the Watersea appeared at all ports, built pyramids and spoke the same gibberish, then vanished. diGriz speaks some similar gibberish back to the sea man.

We hear more fighting from on deck. Someone says "Thou shalt not kill." The sea man in front of us returns on deck. As we head up, they all jump overboard back into the sea. We find Paris dead on deck (and some sea men, killed by the bronzers).

We're taken to the palace and questioned (not interrogated) by a Duke, until Maeglin arrives and chastises the Duke. Apparently these sea men killed Paris, then a tanned human (but still with them) killed the one who killed Paris. We're all free to leave.

Back in Maeglin's suite, we fill him in then he goes to take care of Paris. Almitra comes out of Marlee's room, exits (polymorphs herself) and returns as Marlee. The Emporer's Bird (the 14 year old girl) enters, goes for a private chat with Marisse. Wedge is asked in, and asked to help smuggle out a chest that night. He agrees.

"Marlee" asks Wedge and Marisse to shop for clothes for "Almitra". We go on a big shopping spree until late afternoon. We return, and Almitra dresses.

Wedge and Marisse are thwarted in a sneaking attempt in the castle, but we influence the guards into letting us go. Wedge influences Marisse to trust diGriz; diGriz and Cory walk stealthily to the chest, followed by a unicorn masked in some big illusion. [It's probably Almitra.] They pick up the chest with Tenser's Floating Disk and returns to the group, followed by the unicorn.

diGriz, Shan, Wedge, Marisse, and Almitra trump to Elidor ("Cairn of Stones", trump 91), carrying the chest, and end.

After the adventure, EP is awarded as Elidor Points, given by a "bureaucrat" in a special between-adventures location. Marisse says that in that place, the crow appeared as a man with a big scythe.
