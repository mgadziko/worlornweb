Adventure record - August 14, 1996

diGriz, Shan, Marlee, Wedge, Marisse and the crow begin at the Fountains of Paradise on 1-21-4489 (45 Necrodemus). Marisse and the crow remain dazzled.

diGriz asks Donnerbaum, "How do I get back to where the Monster Tree is in Knock?" Answer: "Find an ozo and trace a monster back the place of its birth." "What's an ozo?" "A magic device. A box for scrying through space and time." "Do you know where one is?" "Yes." "Can you teach me spells for my spellbook?" "No." After much debate with diGriz and viewing Knock (via Threnody's mirror), diGriz asks "Where do you want to go?" Answer: "I want to go where you would have me."

(Meanwhile, Wedge tells Marlee the Tale of Morgan la Faye.)

We all go to the Anarb ranger station (it's in Karli). We get advice from the ranger "Castillo" on where to place the staff: on a tall hill overlooking Anarb, just south of the ranger station. The ranger and friends ask questions of Donnerbaum:

  • Castillo - "Where is the sherrif's treasure hoard?" Donnerbaum tells him.
  • other ranger - "How can Folio be assured of getting his second sword at Orbfest?" Answer: "Death of a person; finding a token."
  • other friend - "Do my enemies suspect my disguise?" "No."

Wedge (believing her an assassin betraying the rangers) kills her with a glance. The two rangers attack. Wedge returns to Fountains with the Word; Marisse kills both rangers. The gods of Tranodeli and Karli whitebrain and circle curse Marlee, Marisse and Shan. diGriz threatens them with destruction and they back down. Meanwhile, Wedge has explained the misunderstanding to Threnody. Wedge trumps to diGriz; the party and all dead are taken to Fountains. Threnody (in full God of Life mode) removes the whitebrains, and restores to life the three Anarb NPCs. All return to Anarb; the gods remove the circle curses.

diGriz plants the staff at the top of the hill. He, Shan, Marlee, Wedge, Marisse and the crow return to the Fountains and end [again, Marisse and the crow are left dazzled].
