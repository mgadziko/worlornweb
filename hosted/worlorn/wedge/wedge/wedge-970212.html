Adventure record - February 12, 1997

?-??-???? alt.Llewghund

Shayol resumes dancing, allows Almitra, alt.Morgan, alt.Wedge and alt.Marisse to join. We're on the ship, docked at alt.Llewghund. Suddenly, redlight falls and remains constant, 24 hours a day. Redlight has never been seen before here.

The Throne of Llewghund is delivered onto shore to the ruins of the castle. Almitra discovers that "everything Almitra left at home" is still there, including the Half-Elven One. Almitra, Morgan and Troy spend weeks rebuilding the castle. Wedge finds some of Almitra's cards, which she can no longer wield. The Buddha's cloak allows him to wield them. Almitra wishes that he would pick them up; he does.

From the water heart, Wedge perceives that he is the focus wielder of Watersea, and that the Turf owner is not present (was Llyr blooded by redlight?) Wedge wishes he knew how to navigate a watersea ship; he receives the skill equivalent of Celestial Navigation 3. Also, it becomes apparent that alt.Wedge is Chaotic Evil in this universe, with some overlain memories of the "real" Wedge.

During the castle's construction, winter falls and lasts beyond its natural period. If not for Troy's spells, the population would starve. Almitra researches her Blood Law spell books. This all takes 244 days.

Meanwhile, Shayol observes Troy and Korish's monk training; they in turn (along with Dare) take dance lessons from Shayol. Wedge summons the watersea ship at speed. All but one are slain from the speed; he slays the "stowaway" when he becomes impudent. He, Shayol and Marisse board and head out to sea. Wedge senses that the Watersea is the origin of both winter and redlight, from a far island with a great elemental force. This island is also where Tanngrisnir is imprisoned. Wedge removes him to a warmer cell; a great cold breath weapon strikes from across the sea. Wedge rolls saves for all.

Over time, the watersea culture becomes Chaotic Evil, under Wedge's influence. In this universe, Marisse is his concubine/bodyguard. He, Shayol, and Marisse return to Llewghund by the end of castle construction. Everyone suspends dreaming; Shayol ends dancing.
