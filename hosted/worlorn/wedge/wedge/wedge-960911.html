Adventure record - September 11, 1996

1-34-4489 (45 Necrodemus); diGriz, Shan, Almitra, Wedge and Marisse on Elidor. Party goal: gain audience with the Prince who was 1000.

We head to the south castle (Almitra says it's Findias). It's a featureless cube in a swamp. Almitra dim doors, diGriz winds the rest of us to the top. diGriz sends a note into the swamp, but there is no response. We all return to the ground.

On the second "day" we notice that time appears not to be passing. We walk toward the western castle (Almitra says it's Falias). The forest we pass through on the way has been burned (by diGriz in the future?). The castle looks like Monticello. We go to the beach, and diGriz sends his note again, but still there is no response.

diGriz and Shan study the destruction for (subjective) days outdoors: the destruction appears to have come from within the castle. Shan expresses concern at Almitra's shapeshifting to diGriz. They play temporal hide-and-seek with a unicorn and a "hunting" party.

The rest of the party study the castle, which is regenerating from the destruction. It is empty and unfurnished. In a closet, Marisse finds a hidden gem, and in the kitchen she finds a container of some black powder (no longer sealed by Time).

Under the centrail dome there is a large sundial, missing its fin. Almitra checks it out, but it throws her back. "It says 'hi'", she says. diGriz and Shan return. Almitra touches the sundial and says "Hi Falias", and is blown back through the wall (there is a unicorn-shaped hole!) Tiresias sells diGriz enough time to go back and prevent her from doing it. (The NPCs are unaware of the timeline change.) Wedge says that legends tell that this castle is called "Morias".

One of the Stone Brothers (the Elidor point "salesman") arrives, and says that Damon Maker ordered that all Stone Brothers find and return "the pomerium sword that Champion made". Wedge claims free will, which surprises the other. Wedge warns him of Yama's enmity toward Stone Brother cooperation (and suggests that the Maker and Deathgod should chat!)

diGriz restrains Almitra in the wind. They argue forcefully, but eventually we decide to stay in the castle. As we end, Marisse finds a sleeping Mewick upstairs -- he wasn't there before.
