Adventure record - August 7, 1996

diGriz, Marlee and Wedge begin in the Fountains of Paradise on 1-20-4489 (45 Necrodemus). Again, someone is trying to gate a fire rat. This time, diGriz lets it work. Paris trump him, and sees him at Thiebaud's Keep, south of New Nostoblet. A mage calling himself Shan the Swordsman (an ex-holdfast leader) offers his services to diGriz. Shan learned of diGriz from Chup. Marlee and Wedge join via trump.

Shan has a gemstone flower (an agate rose) in a rosewood box. As a young man, he bought the agate rose by an old man accompanied by a fire rat. Shan simply wants to travel with diGriz, believing that someday he will meet his younger self and complete the circle. diGriz acquiesces. History: Hal morgantid Shan's love, Dark Agnes. The party returns to the Fountains; Shan (as an elf in "elf-heaven") is dumbfounded.

diGriz asks Donnerbaum more questions: "Where do you want to be?" "Karli." "Do you learn better there?" "Better than most places." "Why?" [Getting this answer took a Roke power] "The Mother talks to me." "What is the limit of your knowledge?" "I don't know today."

We go to Kevin's Watch, to try to gain more insight into Roke and Balance stuff. Wedge's legend lore and Shan's Identify tell that it is a summoning place, with enhanced protections. Shan touches Donnerbaum and confirms that he is in fact the old man who sold the agate rose to his younger self.

Encounter: a woman comes up the stairs, wearing lots of copper. She is bird trained, and enthralls all three males. She ranks their charisma (Shan first, Wedge second and diGriz last), then greets and releases them. Shan seems to have an enmity with birds. Her name is Marisse, and she has come here to try to summon a seagull with a Find Familiar scroll.

diGriz produces a flame, which lights up the pattern in the center of the platform. He then calls up a Flame Strike to summon a fire rat robe. Shan's backpack bursts into flame. He puts it out and pulls out a fire rat robe which he got from a witch in New Nostoblet, and gives it to diGriz.

Marisse begins her summoning. It lasts seven hours, and we stay warm by standing next to diGriz. He, bored, asks Donnerbaum "Will Wedge get laid by this woman?" Answer: "Yes."

After seven hours, a man in a dark cloak appears in the circle, then turns into a crow and goes to Marisse. She names it Orphalese ("eyes wide open") after Almitra's familiar (Marlee had mentioned it). diGriz' Roke training tells him that it is an "angel".

The whole group (diGriz, Shan, Marlee, Wedge, Marisse and the crow) go to the Fountains. Paris leaves Marisse and the crow in their dazzled state, saying that they serve other gods. We end our adventure.
