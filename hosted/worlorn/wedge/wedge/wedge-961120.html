Adventure record - November 20, 1996

Wedge (party leader) and Shayol begin (via 2nd Edition Muse) in Llewghund on 9-9-4493 (49 Necrodemus).

Wedge wants to take Marisse to Bird Keep to get her training for her second sword (as well as a long-overdue "honeymoon" trip :-) Wedge trumps to Aleks at Musquel; we go through. As it's winter, we're reluctant to take wilderness encounters (even if the gate in the white picket fence were open). Aleks informs us that the Winter Man was slain decades ago.

Aleks shows us a sleeping elf named Talin, restored by Josie's invitation. He's Pat Tinker's PC, from the days of Trebor's Lair (and a contemporary of Jil Sander, and Bargold the Nice/Wicked).

Wedge trumps to Diaspar; fails, then Tann; fails. Wedge yells, "Tanngrisner!" across the white picket fence; not response. Wedge returns to Llewghund by its Word. Shayol tries to trump Wedge but can't use them. Aleks trump Wedge via trump, who returns to Musquel. Wedge teaches Shayol the Word for Llewghund. We call for Yama, who opens his mirror for us. We go through and discuss possible ways into Lartroxia. We decide that our best approach is by sea.

We return to the Duchy and Marisse joins us. After 1 day of preparations, we leave on 9-10-4493. We set sail for Isuame using Yama's Greek fishing boat (run by Captain Nemo). After 15 weeks we check in with Aleks, who says it should only be about 11 weeks. Nemo suggests that the boat has no destination. Wedge trumps to Llewella, and asks for info on watersea ports. She says that Isuame is not a port, but Saltpeter's Estate is. We reset our destination, and arrive within 6 hours! We dock on the morning of 3-10-4494 (50 Necrodemus), the planting festival Beltane. Tomorrow is the first day of summer.

Greeted by bronzers, we pay for citizenship: 4K gold for Wedge, Marisse, Shayol and Nemo, plus 50K more for Wedge and Shayol's magic (Marisse pays for her own magic). We part company with Captain Nemo, who returns to the boat.

New news: Isuame is now the Empire's capitol, and Maja Barousse is the Empress! There's been quite a bit of chaos for the last few years, after High Asgar was flooded.

We search for an inn. Shayol makes a critical miss on his Dancer perception roll, so we enter some kind of secret training hall, where human fighters (Manchurian and Florentine) are practicing in pairs with wooden longswords. They lock the door behind us and attack with silver maces.

Marisse and Shayol kick some butt; Marisse with her i, Shayol using open hands. Wedge uses his death gaze one round, then picks the lock and we escape. Marisse and Shayol each grab a mace. Wedge suggests calling for the Bronzers, but is overruled.

We get directions to the Happy Toad from a longshoreman. We meet a truthsay, who gives us correct directions to the Happy Toad. Marisse bumps into a woman and drops the mace, and the bronzers notice. Wedge quickly reports that the party had just been attacked (gasp!) in the city "and thank goodness we finally found the Bronze!" We're all taken in and questioned by a truthsay. Turns out that it was a den of Binder anti-pantheon paladins, so we've done the Empire a service! They put us up at the Black Sapphire, and give us a reward of 30K gold! (Though Shayol and Marisse have to give back the silver maces :-)

Wedge trumps to Aleks; fails, then Llewella; fails. We end the adventure in the Black Sapphire on 3-11-4494.
