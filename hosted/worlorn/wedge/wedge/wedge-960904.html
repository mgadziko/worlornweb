Adventure record - September 4, 1996

1-32-4489 (45 Necrodemus) on Elidor
  • Wedge and Marisse: trump to Paris fails, trump to Threnody fails. Wedge speaks Yama's name attempting to contact him, which kills Marisse and the crow. Wedge goes through to the Paths. Yama says that Marisse and the crow can't stay (some curse) and gives Wedge two good-for-ones. Wedge senses that the Paths are Turf now, and he no longer has any influence there. Wedge goes through to the Fountains; "Good for one alive-and-well crow" and "Good for one alive-and-well Marisse". Threnody (in drow form with a wooden flute) puts both into temporal stasis, and gives each an english pound (to restore their constitutions). He gives Wedge a brach with healing leaves (good till the end of the adventure). He also says that the pomerium is back up good -- we are now isolated from Troad.
  • Back on Elidor with diGriz, Shan and Almitra: two black-dressed elves (Mario and Severain, Morgan's brothers) appear wielding peridot in the party at the tree, then move off toward Gorias. Almitra asks diGriz to warn Saltpeter. He winds there, then walks across the drawbridge. Saltpeter says they were invited, and diGriz returns to the tree.
Wedge and Marisse return via trump to Almitra. We still can't trump Threnody, so Wedge returns to fountains and trumps Almitra to himself. Almitra gives the Maker of Rules to Threnody and he saves her as she returns to human form.

Wedge tells Threnody about "Cory". Wedge trumps diGriz, fails. Almitra looks for Maeglin -- he's returned to Carsultyl. She leaves a bundle of stuff for him (along with a wedding ring) to Paris (Maeglin's teyn). Wedge and Paris talk politics. Maeglin told Paris that a (roke remove) tidal wave engulfed High Asgar. Wedge and Almitra trump back to Elidor (diGriz ensures the probability that we return at the same time he's there).

Almitra realizes that the Maker of Rules has aged her. Wedge cashes in his english pounds (last adventure's ep) in the Paths for TFT talents.

  • diGriz and Shan go to Gorias so diGriz can train for MU levels. Saltpeter is happy and distracted, but he refuses diGriz' request since diGriz is evil. diGriz sends a "message in a bottle" to the Watersea; message returns: "What time is it?"
  • Wedge, Almitra, Duncan and Marisse trump to Maeglin and join him and the High Ones' bird in carsultyl. Duncan makes Maeglin his warlord. Madame Bird "promises" Bird Keep to Marisse. Wedge, Marisse and Almitra trump back to diGriz. We end on Elidor on 1-33-4489.
