Adventure record - November 13, 1996

9-7-4493 (49 Necrodemus). Yama uses his mirror to pull Wedge and Shayol from Llewghund to the Dry Land. Reach is already there. He also uses the mirror to pull Speaker for the Dead from Musquel. Speaker is introduced around.

Yama opens the mirror to Flambeau. Speaker, Reach, Wedge and Shayol go through.

The Childeans see that Wedge has Maeglin's korariel pin, and leave the party alone. We walk along the length of the hall: 7777 wooden beams, each with the life history of one Childean, totalling 41 miles. The long hall is full of Childeans making instruments of war. It takes us two days to walk the length of it. The hall has been divided with "airlocks". The last chamber is a Throne Room.

As we enter, Threnody is conversing with Tanngrisner. A voice in our heads says, "Come closer; I wish you to notice." Tann is not permitted to notice us overhearing. Tann gives (but Thren does not weild) the ORIGINAL-stamped (emerald green) Maker of Rules; Tann retains the Gold Card Maker of Rules fori himself. Threnody: "Do you think Troy is back?" Tann: "As the Prince when he's cogent." Tann leaves.

Threnody to Wedge: SECRET INFORMATION

Threnody to Reach: "How may I be of service?" Reach asks about diGriz' sleep. Threnody asks why Speaker is here. She says "I don't know why. Perhaps to speak for one who is not yet dead." Threnody: "Not yet, but he may die if his hope indeed dies." He gives her a ring with one wish (a 2nd edition magic item).

Shayol begins an interpretive dance around the throne (the Dance of Preparation) "of falling hammers and empty spaces behind stars". Roll=98/85=183 (Threnody has made dice polarity a matter of convenience for Shayol in Flambeau). Threnody: "My first Dancer, why are you here?" Shayol: "To fill the epmty spaces." Threnody: "Do you have empty spaces?" Shayol: "Mine are filling." Threnody tells him of the Senathar, the 97 Steps, and the Scarlet Ribbon. Shayol tells him that his people call it the True Dance (a 50th level spell). Threnody tells him of the Pattern, Kolwynia, Sinanju and Voidcastle, and gives him a Shadow Trump.

Wedge asks if Threnody will add the new adventurers to the trump. Threnody adds (or confirms): Duncan, diGriz, Kannon, Llewella, Barrion 2, Richelieu's Son 2, Almitra, Simon, Reach, Speaker, and Marisse [Eressea must confirm the new position numbers]. He says that Shayol can't be added. "Perhaps Tanngrisner or Fiona could, but it might hurt his Dance." Shayol says that he prefers to walk. Threnody gives him elven food and a decanter of wine. For both, the contents will not spoil.

Threnody to Shayol: "Do not dance when the sky is red until you Know it, since it will Know you."

Threnody puts up a Time Out. Wedge tells him of the PC Yama. Threnody specifically disinvites this Yama. Thren is concerned about the outworld god (Reaper) with Turf. He says Wedge must tell Marlee that Maeglin (because of the Fire Sermon) is a slave of the Reaper. Thren speaks of Joydee's Mask (the Masque of the Red Death); perhaps it would allow someone to impersonate Yama. Time Out over.

Threnody apologizes, but we must all leave via the Foyer. We take the Flambeau-Llewghund link, but are grabbed in mid-transit by Yama back to the Dry Land by his Mirror. Speaker, Wedge and Shayol return to Llewghund; Reach goes to Musquel to be near diGriz, then after a long discussion with Yama, returns and ends in Llewghund.

Speaker, Wedge and Shayol go to the Watersea beach near the Duchy. Speaker (a sea-elf) goes swimming. We see a Wengen surfer out at sea, surfing at the border of Llewghund and the Watersea. Speaker swims out to meet him, they chat. [Surfer shares dreams with Yama] Wedge points his NPC dream wand at the surfer; no response, so it must be a PC. He yells, "Hail, fellow adventurer!" The surfer comes in to converse; it is the Half-Elven One.

Surfing on the edge of the Watersea is causing Llyr's distress. Apparently, the Half-Elven one was made PC last adventure. His plan: after one more adventure, or week of Wasteland time, Llyr will die and Tanngrisner (the Master of Summer) will have to come fight for Monk title. [Editorial note: this Half-Elven One seems a little bonkers; was he always this way?]

Yama gives Wedge a roll of Lifesavers (now he has three!) Wedge, Shayol, Yama and Speaker join Quad and Marlee at the Duchy. All end their adventure on 9-8-4493 (49 Necrodemus).
