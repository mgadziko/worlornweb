The collection below are the Inspired writings from the pen of Halifirien Wedge, one of the most talented of Bardhold's students and teachers. His writings are reproduced nearly intact, and in the manner of their first publication.

His last work, the first here, was written at the passing of his mortal wife. No more recent works by Halifirien Wedge are known.

-Marion Ravenwood, Mistress of Bardhold.
