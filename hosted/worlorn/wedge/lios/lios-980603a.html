My conversation with Dorshal's kin
"Greetings Marcellus. I am the priestess Lanan of the Brein Mark. This is my esteemed aide, Elin Shal, sister of Bladesinger Dor Shal. How are you today?"

"Very well, thank you, and very well met. I am honored and pleased to meet you both, clan and kin of my dear friend Dor Shal. You are travelling far from your home. Have I heard correctly from my friends in Quaal that you have been seeking me and my siblings?"

"Yes, we are far from our homes, but you have heard perhaps erroneous rumors. We do not seek you, but the brother of Elin Shal. We have received ill tidings. Do you have firsthand knowledge you would share with the family of Dor Shal?"

"Yes, you have found precisely the right person. May I ask the ill tidings you have heard, and from whom? Perhaps the rumors you have heard may also be erroneous."

Lanan glances permission to her aide. With a studied look, Elin Shal replies, "Corelan Larethian told me in a holy vision that my brother Dor Shal resides neither on this earth nor in Avranaith. I find this hard to understand. I did not know what to think."

Lanan adds, "There are confirmed rumors that the Braith Holdfast carries the Midnight sword, and this disturbs the Brein also.."

"Indeed. Within my limited understanding, this is what I believe: Your brother made it his task to protect my siblings and myself. When this became impossible to continue in this physical world, he asked for and was granted the ability to continue to serve, by acting as my divine servant."

Lanan: "He is your intermediary?"

"You are correct. My information also is that they cave elves carry the Midnight sword, though not forever."

Elin: "my brother is dead?" Elin swoons, unconscious.

"Lanan, you are precisely correct. We can tell Elin that her brother was slain defending us from the cave elves. It is important for Elin to understand that I believe Dor Shal is not dead; at least not lost to Avernaith. He is here with us now, watching over us, but I believe that when his work is done he will be permitted to complete his journey. "

"Elin is still young in spirit. Although she may have more years of training than you, I sense that you have more insight in to the true nature of the world than she has gained. I understand you are a child of Rilea? You were not then raised in the wisdom of the Quaal Mark? I respectfully suggest we pray together for the miracle of knowledge to permit each other to understand the culture, and upbringing, and background we do not presently share, so that we may be candid and forthright in our continuing discussion."

"I agree with your wisdom. Let us pray."

"Let me share my insights with you and then we shall talk more. Let us pray."

(I set up Dor Shal's bedroll, and ask him to grant me Know Customs, in order for me to understand his people, and for them to understand me). Lanan does her equivalent. Prayers completed, the miracles are cast and cultural knowledge shared.

"My brother Uskar tells me that my Father's hand is on my shoulder."

Lanan frowns, nods. "He is, of course, with us all when we have been granted his divine intervention at our request. You then have the wisdom and skills to invoke divine intervention?"

Marcellus sees that the Brein Marks/culture can not produce greater than seventh level Priests. Marsellus checks his Quaal Mark. The Quaal Mark has wisdom of up to 8th Priests and Priestesses. Some 8th level Priests and priestesses have memories of greater power, but no wisdom from that greater power to hand down to Marcellus.

"At this time, I possess wisdom and skills beyond my own current natural state, granted directly by the hand and will of my Father. So long as I serve His will in an active enough fashion, He continues to answer my direct requests for the grants of powerful miracles."

"Such is known to us. And while I see you did not act with heresy in mind, you now see that Dor Shal's act is unprecedented and ... unnerving, perhaps heretical to the Brein ways. I urge caution who you tell about Dor Shal's sacrifice. There are many in Brein and perhaps other marks who would not be able to see your acceptance of, participation in, and perpetuation of his exile from Avranaith as worthy. We can hope that Elin and her family see things as Dor Shal intended."

Marcellus notes that Dor and Elin are Brein titles, not names.
