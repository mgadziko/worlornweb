Adventure record - June 23 1998

It is the ninth week of winter. Ryndall is asleep and Crimson is missing. Suddenly Crimson appears in our midst.

We hear a knock on the door. Uskar answers it, and sees Mikhail and the ranger Tintagel. Mikhail requests that we accompany him to see Molan's "condition", but resists telling us what that condition is other than to say that Molan kept the peace at great personal cost. Ryndall awakens, and we set off toward a place Mikhail calls "Rosewater", about a month's travel away.

On the first day of spring of our fourth year of wandering, we meet a human who greets Uskar. Uskar explains that he is some sort of "census-taker" (whatever that is!), and asks the man to come back another time. The man agrees and leaves.

We arrive at Rosewater in the third week of spring. There are many kavalar and lios encamped here. There is no sense of war, but the kavalars don't want to let us pass. Mikhail silently holds up his Ivory Sword, and they part before him, and we enter the white picket fence. Inside there is a building. Mikhail asks that we leave our Marks outside before we enter, and we give them to him. We enter the building.

The building appears to be a single large room, with a U-shaped table. Seated around the table are twenty four skeletal corpses of kavalars, each glowing green. There are signs of battle here. Standing in the center is Molan, his eyes glowing green. Slowly he recognizes us, and the green glow fades. He is Undead, something like a banshee or groaning spirit, but his personality still remains. My first instinct is to try to Damn him, but I delay because it is Molan.

He tells us of his plan, to bring the kavalar leaders together under pretext of the lios surrendering. He then slew them all, but not before they killed him. He does not appear to fully realize what has happened to him. Uskar detects an invisible person watching from the other door, who then shows herself. She is human, Khannish from her speech, and one of the pair we saw looting bodies at the battlefield in the Keebler Mark. Uskar and Ryndall converse with her, and say that she seems to be concerned that the "green glow" evil not be allowed to spread, and is ready with magic to "cleanse" the evil. Out of concern for Molan, whom we have hopes of saving, we ask her to wait a while till we have gained as more wisdom about this situation. We go to leave the building, telling Molan that we will be back. He grasps my arms and makes a personal plea that this undead state is not what he wanted.

We leave the building and I lead a cleansing ceremony. We then rejoin the other lios and reclaim our Marks. After a while Uskar and I (and Missy sneakily :-) move off for a private chat with Mikhail, again leaving our Mark's behind. Mikhail explains more of the plan, which involved two other lios Mark Sword weilders and the one-armed kavalar with the Midnight Sword. He also tells that Molan gave his wisdom into his Mark before he entered the building, which is some comfort at least. Besides being a bladesinger, Mikhail is a powerful Swan priest, though he is in need of an Atonement which I cannot give him. He explains that he tried to use a Mark ring to let Molan hear the call of Avernaith, but that it has apparently failed. We explain Molan's Wish (to prevent all war between elves until he has heard the Call), which Mikhail did not know about.

We begin to pray for divination miracles, hoping to gain more wisdom about what to do next. It is the third week of spring of our fourth year of wandering.
