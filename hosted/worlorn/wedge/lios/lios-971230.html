Adventure record - December 30, 1997

Successfully assimilate Khansman's knowledge.

Charlie tells of "Tascom the Hunter", and we can get more info from the furrier. Charlie doesn't know of an inn or tavern with a breathing bull, but he can get more information from others in his merchant train.

We go to the furrier's -- Tascom will be in town tomorrow. Charlie pays some kids to tell us when he arrives. We return to the inn and let Charlie search for more info for us. Missy speaks with her bag bag, and trades it 20 gold for a steel sword it says it just remembered it had. It barfs up a red-tinted steel katana -- Crimson says that it is imperial contraband, possession of which is punishable by death. Fernan tests it; it is exceptionally sharp.

We're bothered by a small drunken bunch of ruffians, but the bronzers chase them away. Charlie returns in the evening with a Gorgon clasp, a Necrodemian house of non-kav, non-lios elves in a wood named Sherwood. Charlie says that gorgons don't follow Nolerak.

Next day, we wait at the tavern. An urchin comes in for his copper and we head for the furrier. Tascom is there. He's a half-elf (taller than even Uskar, with elvish features) with a horseload of skins, selling them. Once he's done, he comes over to us. He and Ryndall chat (Ryndall uses Oriel's introduction), and agree to continue discussions out of the fortress. We go back to the inn for Fernan and sleeping Uskar and leave town (saying bye to Charlie).

Ryndall bargains for pipeweed. Tascom teaches Marsellus "cherry tobacco" and "marijuana", and tells Crimson about rogue organizations. Tascom is unreligious. He gives us information about travel from here to Wunib, and draws us a rough map. He leaves, heading north, and we head south along the river. Two weeks we reach a bridge across the river, which we don't use. Two more weeks and the road leaves the river; we continue following the river. At night, we dream of music calling us to the mountain south. We recross the road and the call ends. We deduce that Tascom was misinformed and head back north two weeks and cross the river at the road direct to Wunib. The wisdom of the Swan vanish as we cross over.
