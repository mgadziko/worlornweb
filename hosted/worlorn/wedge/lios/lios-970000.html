Adventure record - Background

Marsellus is a wood elf (lios elfar: "elves of light"), son of Releya and (she says) Nolerak, god of the lios. He, with his older brother Ryndall, twin sister Mystea, and younger brother Ooskar grew up in Thornycroft Grove, isolated from other elves and knowledge of the outside world.

Marsellus' faith in his mother, and his childhood discovery of a necklace which she told him had belonged to his father, inspired him to gain "wild" and untutored abilities as a Priest to Nolerak. He tends to be a little rash, and may rush to judge situations he does not fully understand. His youthful haste sometimes causes him problems in achieving the calmness needed to effectively invoke miracles.

In recent years, Marsellus perceived that Releya was becoming discontent, and he feared that she might soon leave them for the lios' heaven of Arvanaith. Then one morning, she brought all her children together to tell them that Nolerak had come to her in a dream, saying that her children had a great destiny ahead of them. She instructed them to leave Thornycroft Grove, and spend ten years visiting the other Marks: Quaal, their neighbors to the west, Kestrel, east over the mountains, Keebler, north past the desert, Brein, far in the northeast, and Swan, far in the west. Ten years to gain wisdom, and then return to her.

It was with considerable trepidation that Marsellus took his first step outside the Grove. The occasional visitor from nearby Quaal Mark had told stories of the humans and their dark cities, stories which frightened him, but his love and concern for his mother, and his faith in his god and father Nolerak helped him put aside his fear, and his first step was followed by a second, and a third...
