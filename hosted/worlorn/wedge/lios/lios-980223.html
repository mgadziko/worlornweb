Adventure record - February 23, 1998

Over the course of a month (5 weeks):
  • The Quaals work a lios mark into Ryndall's redsteel katana.
  • Ryndall constructs an exceptional bow from some horn that Molan has. The mages enchant it to legendary status as a gift for Missy.
  • Uskar constructs frames for his red crystal lenses, for convenient wearing.
  • The Quaals gift us (except Crimson) with quaal cloaks and boots.

Missy (Jil Sander?) wakes up at the beginning of the ninth week of autumn and receives the bow. Fernan stays behind to reclaim her hand (her students).

Uskar sees through the glasses that Molan's arm has its hand, and cannot see the hand that I carry. I look through the glasses, and see the man from the flames, standing very close. I drop the glasses in alarm! Crimson asks to see, and sees five hobbits milling about with a human child. When they notice him they wave, but when he starts concentrating they disappear.

Molan accepts Ryndall's invitation to join his hand, with the proviso that Ryndall will honor Molan's first request to leave for personal reasons with a year and a day of the request. Fernan gifts Molan with the Oaken Sword, and gifts Crimson with a small magical silver dagger.

Missy shaves her head (to help conceal this body's age) with Fernan's assistance before we leave.

We part with Fernan, and travel five weeks in the plains toward a human city called the Draalar Duchy, until we reach the asphalt road, staying in existing Quaal trees each night. Molan uses his magic to ensure our travel is private, hidden from other Quaals. During our travels, Ryndall picks up combat tactics from Molan.

At the road, Uskar uses his glasses and sees invisible creatures travelling the road very quickly. Suddenly, a human woman runs from the forest, screaming. She is being chased by a hand of ogres, and Uskar recognizes one as a mage. She runs to us and begs for help, calling herself "Tamara". Ryndall attacks the mage, while Missy, Crimson, and Molan attack the others. They make quick work of the ogres. Crimson slays one extremely gruesomely from behind with his nine-bladed sword, but receives a serious blow from its companion.

It is the second week of winter, one week's travel from the Duchy.
