Adventure record - December 17, 1997

I've been asleep for a while; it's full spring. Here's what happened while I was asleep according to my siblings and Crimson:

Uskar sang the song; 20 Swans showed up and gave marks to each elf
-- met Roweena the Swan, Danielle, male who replaced Mikhael; Raphael, female; Oriel, male; Lilliel, female (Mikhail was in her hand; he's the 2nd youngest swan)
-- they are all old; have had no children in a long time and have not been called away
-- then they left

Our hand left and headed for the Khan's Fort (a human lair where Crimson hopes for training). Crimson met a Kestrel while on patrol at night. he was killing 4 Braith and was avoiding others. He gave Crimson five marks. He called himself "Chanson" in a human language (it means "singer"). Then Crimson met two humans, a man and woman of Shando who said they would dispose of the bodies.

Then a faerie elf named Napolean came into camp, conversed with Ryndall and Missy. He said that if Marsellus brewed some beer at Wunib we would get safe to passage to Keebler's Tree.

Big plan-- head east toward the Keebler Mark. Then Dorshal appears to the siblings and motions us north. We quickly meet 4 female lios with spears: "I am winter". "spring" "autumn" "summer" they say (very old; brown hair). Missy gives a swan mark to me. Winter says "you need not fear us". Missy shows Marsellus the swan ritual of food, blessing then feeding each other. Autumn has a CG "city" holy symbol. Winter has an acorn. All speak in CG in the true name of nolerak. "Our leader is Magda; she named them" Summer speaks the least and is oldest.

We discuss religious dogma over breakfast (mostly with Autumn): revenge; no swan has been called since X; she perceives Dorshal. We hold a parting ceremony, then head back east for a day. Some kavs warn us away from quarmal's mountain.
