Adventure record - November 18, 1997

Missy and Uskar awake! We bring them up to speed and continue to "the singing place". We assist a travelling human "Cuthbert" to fix his wagon at a ford. He gives us a ride in his wagon (at magical speed) -- without him, we could never have made it in time. Cuthbert appears to be a Lawful Good priest.

"Know Age" on Cuthbert: 4444 (!) ie, off-calendar; he tells Fernan that he knew the Quaalite two generations before her father (he says that he hasn't seen all that subjective time). Missy goes to sleep. Cuthbert says the master of redlight acts against those friendly toward the winter man. He has both coven and priest abilities. Ryndall realizes that the Swan are intentionally avoiding us. We arrive at the Mound of Spring; this is where Cuthbert saw Mikhail get his sword (when he was too young to deserve it and too unwise to return it, so Cuthbert says).
