Adventure record - April 11, 1998

Suddenly, Crimson Gray appears in Uskar's place! Neither Nightingale nor the Ironjades notice anything amiss in this. Crimson and we lios are confused, but the Ironjade priest insists that we begin moving, and says that we will be travelling for about two hours.

Road encounters:

  • We avoid "Norris, who needs no weapon", a human wearing a small six-pointed star.
  • No apparent encounter.
  • The priest picks up some sort of rock.
  • Another Ironjade picks up something and hands it to the priest.
  • We surprise five women ("dagger ladies") arguing over some corpses. Missy and Crimson attempt to pick pockets while the Ironjades and Ryndall move to attack. Ryndall slays one and one of the Ironjades slays another. Crimson successfully picks the pocket of one of the women.

    The Ironjades are upset at the sight of Ryndall's redsteel weapon, and avoid us. We all (except) for Molan avoid as well, which leaves him in combat with the three remaining women. He slays two, but one avoids. He is very severely wounded, so I give him some first aid and help him walk. I take the silver chain linked pair of obsidian daggers that

  • Missy picks up a chicken.
  • We avoid some Gorgon samurai. Molan seems unable to avoid, and says that he has forgotten his songs.
  • We meet a dozen human bat samurai. Crimson and Ryndall avoid, but Missy and I stay with Molan. They flourish and bow; they seem to recognize her as this "queen". We pass on.
  • We see a red giant (Redsteel Golem) running down the road toward us. Ryndall avoids, and when the giant gets to where Ryndall was, he snaps his fingers and goes away.
We get off the road and set up a Quaal tree. We go inside, and begin ministering to Molan. He falls into a comatose sleep. Over the next day, Ryndall supplies us with food while I cure Molan. Even fully healed he doesn't awaken, so I pray and Dorshal grants me Remove Curse and Cure Disease. Remove Curse has no effect, but upon receiving Cure Disease he begins to breathe easier. After an evening of natural sleep he enters reverie, then awakens. He has lost some abilities I also finish curing myself and Ryndall.

Molan tells of his subjective experience: We left Uskar in town studying with a sage, and Crimson came with us to the cavern. Crimson was with us in the Dragon Piss Inn. Crimson tells that he remembers going to sleep on Zeepsday evening of the first week of Spring, then woke up standing with us on the Road. We believe it is Zeepsday evening of the last week of Winter. We are all mightily confused. Crimson tells that stories have been told over the week about lios slaying Shanagates, so it would not be safe for us to go back.

I tell Molan more explicitly how Missy took Jil Sander's curse, so that Jil could return to Nolerak in Avernaith. Molan judges that given this boon, Jil Sander would not begrudge Missy taking over her place as Queen of Garfieldland. He recommends returning to Pyethorn when we can, in order to tell the Quaal of this.

We have spent a total of 2 days in the tree. We believe it is Saturday morning, the last day of winter. Missy and Crimson go back into the city to make an arrangement with Tamara to pick a safer place to meet Uskar. Molan, Ryndall and I go to the Cairn of Spring (a nearby shrine of an old battle between Shanagates and Quall), where Molan believes we can find some Shanagate artifacts that may help us appease the kavs, if necessary.

In town, Crimson finds that Tamara's memories are very different from his. She remembers that when we all brought her to Ashertiri, Crimson left with us lios and did not stay to learn about the city's thieves' guilds. She says that is is Saturday, the last day of winter.

I direct Ryndall to dig while Molan points out the important gear, while I chant, telling the spirits that the gear is being returned to their holdfast in order to placate them. We obtain five holdfast blades, five pairs of wristguards, and five pairs of chainmail. Ryndall discards his gloves afterwards and receives the blessing of Nolerak from me. We return to the tree.

In town, Tamara, Crimson and Missy go to a restaurant called The Ivory Cup to chat. Tamara says that Uskar is studying with a sage named Chantilly, and agrees to set up The Ivory Cup as our meeting place with Uskar when he is done with Chantilly. Tamara has to use a powerful divination magic to get the information she needs, then has four extra questions she can answer for Crimson:

  1. Who put Crimson Gray on the Traveller's Road? The Traveller.
  2. Who changed Crimson Gray's timeline? The Traveller.
  3. Can the timeline of Marsellus of Nolerak and Crimson Gray be restored? Yes.
  4. How can that timeline be restored? Take the road back.

She says that The Traveller was the source of her information. The answers were actually "Me! (laughter)", "Me! (laughter)", "Yes", and "Take my road back." Then Missy and Crimson return to the tree.

Tomorrow morning will be Spring Begins of our third year of travel.
