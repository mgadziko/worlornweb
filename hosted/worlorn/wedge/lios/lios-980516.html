Adventure record - May 16, 1998

I wish that Missy had her experiences restored that were lost to the faerie's throwing star. The Mark ring I carry now has only 1 leaf remaining. We wait three weeks till Missy's catalog order arrives. By that time, everyone is in full health. It is two weeks into autumn.

The living wood door to the dungeon has repaired itself. Crimson climbs up on top of the building and investigates the roof, but finds nothing interesting. Uskar (there is only one!) is asleep. Nostradamus is willing to guard him in our absence, and we trust him for no good reason. Nostradamus tells that he had a vision that foretold that he was destined to meet someone like Uskar. He left the city, with no food or water, and wandered until he met us.

Missy looks for the Midnight Blade in the scrying ball. She sees it on the belt of One Arm. He is in a Braith place with other Braiths. They are sitting in stone chairs around a stone table with maps on it, arguing. She breaks contact, waits for an hour and a half, then scrys again. This time, one of the other kavs notices and breaks the contact.

We feel like we're being watched. Missy asks me to cast Dispel Magic, which removes the feeling. Within five minutes, we feel it again, but it goes away by itself in a round. Missy asks Nostradamus who was scrying us. He takes two weeks to reveal to us that it was "the One-Armed King of the Green People". Over this time we occasionally feel the scrying, so it appears that they are watching us. I guess that they are planning a war, and worried that we've seen their plans. Nostradamus says that green is the color of their evil magic, and that the Braith are the instrument of the invasion of the Prime Material. Nostradamus' home and library are very far away, north and east. Ryndall finds a Quaal party and passes on the warning about the Braith planning bfor war, which takes two days.

I prepare to cast the miracle of Commune, by making some holy water over several days. I also cleanse the gear that people got in the dungeon, so that Missy can identify it. We retire within the lands of the Quaal, in the fifth week of autumn.
