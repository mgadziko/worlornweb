Dreams of light...

Lately, I've been having odd and intermittent dreams. I dream that I'm one of the wild wood elves, the lios elfar, the Elves of Light. It's hard to remember the dreams, especially the early ones, because this elf's thoughts are so strange and foreign. He thinks in terms of the colors of the suns, the shade of the trees, the sounds of the forest creatures, in ways that are difficult to understand.

As these dreams have continued, I've begun to take notes each morning of the impressions I can recall. I don't know whether these dreams are real, or whether my creative muse is unsatisfied with only driving me during daylight, but I share them now with you...

Halifirien Wedge
