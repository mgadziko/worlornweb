Adventure record - February 9, 1998

Missy tells of her vision:
She, Jil Sander, and Dorshal were there. Missy heard the musical call of Nolerak, but Jil did not. Jil told Missy that she had been following us, hoping to find a way to return to Nolerak. Dorshal indicated that Missy should go to the sea. She feared to go alone, and took Jil with her. At the ocean, Nolerak was on a ship. He said, "There is room for only one." Missy indicated that Jil should go. He said, "The Children of Nolerak are strong. Jil Sander, will you come with me?" Jil heard the call and entered the ship. He shed a single tear, then they sailed over the ocean.

Uskar detects that Missy's sword is evil, and that he cannot find her with his scrying crystal. While resting, we discuss Misty and her fate. There is some disagreement and doubt amongst the party, but we come to no firm conclusion. I believe that it is really Misty, and Fernan also seems inclined that way; the others harbor doubts, especially Uskar.

We leave the tree by the village and return to the tree where Uskar left the ring and disk. Uskar retrieves the ring and we return to the river. An ogre and an orc are guarding the bridge, one using Ryndall's own ordinarium blade (stolen when we were captured); Ryndall and Fernan charge to attack. Fernan's Oaken Sword turns the orc into a tree rooted in the riverbed! This causes the bridge to partly collapse. All three fall into the river and the rest of us run to help. Fernan can swim and keeps Ryndall from drowning; Uskar sends the disk out to save them.

The bridge is sufficiently functional for us to cross, and we are back in the Swan Mark. I plant another tree and we retire within the lands of the Quaal.
