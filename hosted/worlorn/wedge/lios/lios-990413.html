Adventure record - April 13, 1999

In the treehouse of Nony, the swan sage, in Berolyn town. It is two week's until winter begins. Anael, Uskar, Missy, Ryndall, Crimson, and Derek are here. Tanri is not present, and none of the "For's" are present.

Crimson wakes up screaming -- the wooden platform he's sleeping on is on fire! It's been raining, so the platform is a bit damp, and the rain is putting the fire out. It appears that a very hot object suddenly appeared near him. The (cooling) object looks like the remains of a charred humanoid.

Nony (who reveals that she is a priest) and I notice that the human is actually still alive, though this should be impossible with such severe burns. When I try to heal it, I realize that it is some kind of supernatural creature which is immune to my miracles. Using his magic spectacles, Uskar sees that it as a woman with bat-like wings. He attempts to detect evil, but detects none.

Nony performs the miracle of Know Alignment, which reveals the burnt woman to be of Chaotic Good alignment. Uskar uses his silver dagger to Heal her. Her burns vanish and she is whole, but she is still unconcious. He now recognizes her as the woman from a dream he had, calling her "the ninth counselor", and we recognize her as the priestess Carlos called upon to heal Molon's severed arm.

On intuition, Uskar clairvoys back to the tree where we left the sleeping Tanri, and sees that she is now awake. Anael flies back to retrieve her (it will take two days, there and back). Tanri is met by Richelieu's Son's Son, who tells her that her friends are OK and where we were heading.

While we're waiting for Anael to return with Tanri, Uskar studies Nony's books. I estimate that the woman will remain unconcious for a hand of hands of days. Missy decides she wants to stay with the unconcious woman and nurse her back to health, asking us to leave her behind when we continue our travels. Nony's treehouse has a shrine to Nolerak, and I Commune with Dorshall about the wisdom of leaving Missy behind for this purpose; he says it's OK.

When Anael, Tanri, and Richelieu's Son's Son arrive, he tells us the news that the Quaal has heard the Song of Avernaith, and has been succeeded by Fernan Solar!

Tanri asks Nony for information about the Swan, and Lorien in particular, saying that she's researching for a song she's writing for Lorien. Nony and Son's Son know of various private journals that Lorien wrote for "him", about 63 books each covering about a year. Nony reveals that the Swan are waiting for Lorien to sing the song of Avernaith for them. Missy (a little rashly) says that Lorien died at the hands of Slavers, which shocks Nony. Nony seems to think that Tanri plans to try to sing the song in Lorien's place -- Tanri says, "I won't be singing instead of Lorien. I'll be singing for her." Nony decides to abandon her library, to go to the singing She'll turn the treehouse library over to Uskar. Within two days, she's gone.

Ryndall realizes that his Swan Mark (given to him by Lorien), is really Lorien's Mark, containing her wisdom. He works with Tanri to share its stories with her. She works on her song over the course of the next 30 days or so. At one point, Amar passes by, now with a pack of wolf companions.

The unconcious woman wakes up one day, just after redlight. To Missy she says, "You remind me of Jil Sander". She recognizes Crimson as Kin, and introduces herself as Morgan. She says she needs to get to something called a "Life Hutch" before the next redlight. She wants to use Crimson's Trump, but can't because she's something called "Dust Law" and he's "Blood Law", whatever those are.
