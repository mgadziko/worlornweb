Adventure record - January 28, 1998

We spend two days healing and preparing. Missy looks for the missing mark blade with the scrying crystal. She sees a group of 8 orcs travelling rapidly, two gravely wounded; much of their loot (our possessions) appears damaged by heat, including the sword. We decide to pursue them, to take back the sword. Uskar buries the disk's control ring and Ryndall carries Crimson so he can track the orc party at full speed.

When we reach the (former) orc village, we see that it has been destroyed by fire, apparently from four strikes. Ryndall sees signs of battle, in which the orcs eventually defeated an undetectable foe. Uskar uses his magic to detect the invisible creature, lying motionless. Feeling around, he says that seems to a corpse with no bones. He finds a sword and frees it from the hand, whereupon it becomes visible.

Missy gets an evil gleam in her eyes (as I have seen from possessed creatures), and grabs the sword. Ryndall gets the same evil gleam and strikes at Missy, trying to take the sword. I interpose and try to restrain him. He strikes me, but then comes to his senses.

Meanwhile, Missy speaks to her bag bag, which says that it doesn't know who she is. She plays with the sword a bit, slicing trees easily. After she sheathes it, Ryndall grabs Missy and restrains her while I take the sword away from her. She fights, and Ryndall carries her away to a nearby stream and throws her in. She also comes to her senses, and the evil gleam goes away.

At her first glimpse of the sword, Fernan turned away and began doing katas to concentrate her will to resist the call of the evil sword.

Missy says that a female elf is imprisoned in the sword. She speaks to her bag bag, which ate all her gold when it thought she was gone. It's sick and throws up a bunch of booty, some Missy's and some from previous owners. She begins going through the stuff, and takes some down to the river to wash it off. Distracted by helping Uskar examine the undetectable corpse, neither Ryndall nor I notice. Uskar discovers that the corpse is wearing some kind of invisible suit and removes it, revealing the boneless body of an elf woman.

When we notice Missy's absence, Ryndall goes looking for her. Her finds her floating face down in the stream with a poisonous green color. He carries her back to the rest. She is wearing a cursed poisonous cloak. For hours, we all try everything we can think of to help, but it is too late. She is dead.

In a vision, I see Dorshal holding Missy's shoulder, keeping her (for now?) from moving toward a Light. An evil creature offers to bargain for her life, but I do not respond to it. It appears the same as the evil cigarette-smoking creature from the Salvador Deli.

Uskar feeds the evil sword to the bag bag. He and Ryndall say that Missy's Mark blade is holding her spirit. Fernan collapses from exhaustion from hours of katas, and we are all in shock.
