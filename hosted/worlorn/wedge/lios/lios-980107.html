Adventure record - January 7, 1998

On our own in the Keebler Mark, the four siblings with Fernan Solar and Crimson Grey. Uskar finally wakes up and we fill him in, though Missy is asleep.

Crimson lends his Keebler Mark (an ivory cookie) to Ryndall, but there is no Ranger wisdom in it. Uskar checks, and there is Mage wisdom -- perhaps there has ever only been one Keebler Ranger, named Rambo. He is Keebler's "ferret", and gives out the Keebler arrows. Fernan says that the Keebler sword (the Emerald sword) has long been lost. Uskar's wisdom says that this land is Shando, full of faeries and other magical creatures. The Keebler elves themselves all live in the Forest Within The Forest. Based on this information, we decide that we do, in fact, need to go to Wunib.

That night while Marsellus is on watch, he is approached by a woman who says we are trespassing in her grove. She also says that she wants to "cleanse" any magic sword. She vanishes into a mist and attacks with a cold beam of magic when she sees Ryndall's anti-magic sword, which severely wounds Rydall, Fernan, and Crimson. Fernan makes an exceptional strike with her wooden sword and the woman is transformed into an oak tree which she proceeds to chop up.
First Aid on Crimson
Cure Light Wounds

Missy wakes up. Fernan makes a campfire, burning the tree to prevent regeneration. She believes it was an ogre mage. In the distance, we hear a scream after many bow-string twangs. After, the one-armed kav with the saunters up to us, saying he has us ambushed. Ryndall: "I wish it were the will of Nolerak that this not happen this night". It is morning and we are rested.

(Crimson sees us freeze. Two dozen Braiths ignore him and attempt to kill the elves, but the siblings are statues and cannot be affected; all except Uskar who is limp, but any who molest him are struck by lightning. The kavs rough Crimson up. He hears them call the green glow from Ryndall's ring hand "the light of the lord" and "lochnar". They are _very_ angry, but eventually leave.)

Misty sets up her Tree and we "retire within the lands of the Quaal"
Accelerate Healing on Ryndall
Accelerate Healing on Fernan
3 Cure Light Wounds on Crimson

Uskar detects that the remaining green discoloration in Ryndall's hand is evil, after he discovers evil legends of "lochnar" from the wisdom of the lios eldars. Uskar uses his magic crystal to view the one-armed braith, who is in full formal attire with wolf-head symbols. He (the braith) is outdoors, pacing in anger. His underlings are burning their dead with green flame.

We hear a gong sound outside our tree. Uskar uses magic to see what appears to be a female swan, old but with blond hair, sitting with three swords. In the distance is smoke. Missy opens the tree, and the woman tells us that the kavs will come back after they finish their ritual. She says she will hold off the kavs, and Ryndall leads us back over the river into the lands of the Swan. Ryndall recognizes her as a half-elf.

Unfortunately, there is a group of braith crossing the bridge toward us, so we retire back into the forest. Fortunately we lose them. Unfortunately, we run into an encampment of 10 hands of braith while gabbing loudly. We are suddenly surrounded by Brein Mark lios who are here for their sword.
