Adventure record - June 16 1998

It is the seventh week of winter. We continue on toward Hobbiton, joined by the bard Kirok. Once in the town proper, he smooths our way with gold until we are brought to the Chamberlain in the palace. After some verbal swordplay, the Chamberlain agrees to teach Crimson some rudiments of Kin lore, and shows Crimson his own card in the Deck. He also expresses his irritation that some hobbit in black has entered Hobbiton recently and is causing him trouble.

Rather than wait, we elves return to Bardhold where Kirok introduces us to the caretaker, Windhaven. She welcomes us, and Kirok's information about the Chamberlain being Kin, but is peeved to learn that he knows she herself is Kin (and knows where she keeps her own Deck!)

Meanwhile, Uskar awakes back in Mom's house. Nostradamus is nowhere in sight. Uskar scrys and sees him far away, talking with two kavalar elves. Suddenly, a tree outside explodes in flame and a small demon walks out. (Local coven put out the flames.) He was sent to answer three questions of Uskar's, as a way of delivering messages from some other diviner. After some slightly impolite verbal swordplay, Uskar gains the intended knowledge, then the demon leaves. The message purports to come from the diviner who trained Uskar in Ashertiri. Apparently she is dead, but wants to come back from the dead without being evil and hopes that Uskar can help. He scrys her himself, and she appears alive and perfectly well in her lab.

We regather and return to Mom's house, to find Uskar waiting for us while doing spell research. He fills us in, and we fill him in. We discuss whether we should go to Nostradamus' aid, or the diviner's aid, or neither. It is the ninth week of winter.
