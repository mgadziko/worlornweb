Adventure record - December 8, 1998

early autumn, Maja's mansion in Isuame

Misty, Tanri and Marsellus awake. Derek, Crimson, Ryndall, and Uskar remain asleep. Tanri, Derek, and Crimson each have a small, monkey-like, companion dressed identically to them. Tanri seems to recognize hers from a dream...

Maja comes in to announce that two pair of Braith kavalar have arrived and want to speak to the lios. We three, with Tanri's little friend, go down to meet them, with Misty speaking for me. The Braith are highbond, dressed in very ornate armor, and appear to be quite old. They return the gear and personal ornaments from some lios who've died in battle with the Braith. They assure us that the lios did indeed die, and were not tainted by the the evil green glow. The gear is mostly of the sort associated with the Brein Mark, including some actual marks. [We later recognize them as from the battle we saw in western Shando last year, and then recall that it was Maja and Sharra that we saw "looting" the bodies afterwards.] It appears that returning the gear is very meaningful to the Braith. The Braith want to know what we know about Somana, and Misty tells them some of the story. They are grateful, and make nice with us, giving Misty a large box of coins before leaving.

After the Braith are gone, Maja and Sharra join us. Sharra notices Tanri's little friend and suddenly leaves the room, dragging Maja with her. Tanri tries to communicate with the little person, but "it" (she?) only seems to know a few phrases: "I am for Tanri", "Don't hurt Tanri", "Isle of Sleep", "Sharra, fire place", "githyanki", "Morren". Eventually, Maja and Sharra return. Maja uses a miracle to speak with the little person. Maja says that she has been rejected by her people for allowing Tanri to be killed through inaction. We notice that the little person (she apparently has no name) has the signs of a coven "circle curse" on her forehead. Sharra can speak to her in Lawful Evil alignment tongue.

A moral debate ensues. :-)

Maja mentions that these creatures have some kind of probability control, so we speculate that she and her counterparts "for" Derek and Crimson may be very useful to have along in the Wizard's Tower.

As I spend time cleansing the Brein Mark gear, and Misty studies magic spells with Maja, Tanri and her little friend begin to learn each others' language. Tanri also begins harp lessons for her little friend. Two months remain before Poric will leave the area of the Wizard's Tower.
