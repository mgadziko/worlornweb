Adventure record - February 4, 1998

Uskar tells us that Missy's wisdom is incompletely held in her Laurethian mark blade, and also her swan and quaal marks, and will fade in a few days because she had no time for the proper ceremonies.

We decide to burn the other elf, then bury Missy where she fell, and plant one of the trees of the Quaal. This will honor Missy in the Quaal way, by letting her untimely death still participate in the cycle of life. Fernan (with her Oaken Sword) and I will perform the ceremony of burial. While Fernan prepares, we will burn the other elf.

While the pyre burns, a long-haired, supernaturally beautiful, human-formed fire creature appears over the dead elf. He says to us, "Is she finished in this world?" I reply "Yes". She immolates instantly. He comes out of the flames, and takes a normal human form. "Was she beloved?" "We owe her our lives, but she was not known to us." He names her Jil Sander, one of the Seventeen. He says, "Your father took her personally. Do you want me to bring her back to you?" Wisdom of the Quaal: he is the Master of Redlight. He names himself Joiry. Conversing with Crimson, he calls him a "Time Bandit". He appears to be familiar with Crimson from other times, and is jokingly antagonistic. Fernan makes googly eyes at him, and is quite (uncharacteristically) enamored.

I tell Joiry yes. He says he'll need the sword. I go rouse Uskar from meditation, and have him bring the bag bag. He opens the bag, and it responds with Missy's voice!

Missy is possessing the bag from the sword -- she says that Jil is with Nolerak, and she took Jil's place in the sword. Joiry asks Uskar for the bag, then turns it inside out (making a green bouncer) and the sword falls to the ground. Joiry asks Missy for the sword, and she gives it to him. He goes to Missy's body, puts the sword in her hand and casts spells (he seems to be his own god and divine servant). Her body becomes Jil's body. Joiry names the blade "Cuisinart". Jil has long since desired to return to Nolerak, but her Great Weapon held her back. It would appear that Missy's death experience was necessary to resolve this ancient tragedy.

Joiry tells the bouncer that he's taking it back to "The Forbidden Isle" as a guardian. He names "Seth Cello-son" as the cigarette smoking man, and says that they are enemies, "Blood" and "Dust".

Joiry takes his leave with the bouncer close behind, while Fernan sighs after him.

Missy's new body is very old (utterly black hair). It's her body's extreme old age that makes her appear as not lios. Ryndall's legends say that Jil Sander led orcs and dragons against Shanagate Holdfast.

We retire within the lands of the Quaal.
