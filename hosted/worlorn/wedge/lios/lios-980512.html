Adventure record - May 12, 1998

It is two day's before Midsummer.

Crimson had a strange dream. I, Ryndall, Missy, and Uskar had a similar dream. Ryndall, Missy, and I share our versions of the dream.

We decide to go to Ashertiri to obtain the material component for a miracle that may help us regain Crimson's nine-bladed sword.

As we exit the tree, we see a Swan who addresses Molan. When Molan directs him to speak to his thumb (Ryndall). The Swan is flabbergasted, but introduces himself as "Artael". Artael brings a message for Molan. He draws a sword and says "Mikhael will receive you at a place of your choosing." Molan responds "I do not choose to meet him." Artael takes his leave, glancing back at us in confusion as he goes.

After two weeks travel, we reach Ashertiri. (During the journey, local Quaals tell Ryndall that some Brein priests are in the area, asking for us and news of Dorshall.) Crimson goes into town to buy gloves, while Molan goes also on some private errand of his own.

Coming into town at night, Crimson and Molan meet the woman from Crimson's dream, who apparently doesn't recognize him. She is travelling with some of the priest women whom we met guarding the large golden magical transport disk. Crimson says, "Lilith". He says that he thinks he's her cousin and that he saw her in a dream. She replies "Cousin,eh? I've killed those before." Then she clarifies that she prefers to be called "Eve", in this time and place. She nicks him and tastes his blood, and calls him "Cousin".

Molan attempts to intervene, but Lilith casts a spell on him which puts him into some kind of trance.

She asks Crimson about his dream. He tells that she took him up a mountain in a chamber. She says, "I took you to Kolvir?" He affirms, and then says that they met Singer at the top by a Pattern. She asks "Do you wish for the other cousins to be aware of your presence? Have you been given Trump? Do you know how to use them?" He says "No and Yes". She takes out a deck of cards and puts hers on top. She says that so long as he is unknown to any other cousins, she will respond to his call. She asks whether he knows his lineage, and he says "It's complicated." She responds "Isn't it always?" He asks "How will I know who is on our side?" She responds "My dear Crimson Gray. You and I are on our side. Everyone else is an enemy." She walks away, leaving him with a blank handkerchief with silver embroidery of a spider in one corner and a bat in the opposite corner, to dab his slight wound.

Crimson awakens Molan, who is surprised to see Crimson alive at all. Molan checks him for bite marks, and finding none says "You're lucky!" Molan says that she is the Queen of all Drow. Molan asks whether he has any bite marks. Crimson checks and there are none.

Crimson and Molan continue into town. Molan picks up his boots, then they split up to their own errands. At his guild, Crimson discovers that the hobbit woman who stole his nine-bladed sword is named "Kara", and is legendary. He's told that "She's so scary that Spam won't even let her back in!" and that she's a travelling person, and that Crimson probably won't see her again unless she wants to be found. He also learn that Shanagates are still in town, and more have come (70 or so), searching for more information about the elf party. The fact that they're still looking information means that they haven't found any. He hears a rumor of a nine-bladed sword in New Nostoblet. Incidentally, he also obtains a pair of hobbit-sized black silk gloves.

The next morning Crimson rejoins Molan, and then they rejoin the rest of us. Two weeks of travel brings us back to the Llabyrinth. Ryndall sees signs that Brein priests were here looking for us, but they are gone now.

I cast Continual Light on a silver brooch of Uskar's. Then we end in the tree, to make plans for re-entering the llabyrinth to search for Crimson's sword.
