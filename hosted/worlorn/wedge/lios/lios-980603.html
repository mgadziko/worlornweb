Adventure record - June 3, 1998

When I awaken, it is the fourth week of winter, two weeks before Midwinter's Day. Since my discussions with the hand of Brein Mark lios, they have brought me near Ashertiri, where my siblings are waiting for me.

While I spent time with the Brein, they journeyed down to Ashertiri. Uskar obtained mage training in the city. Then he learned that some of the magical tomes he had obtained from Doctor Scofflaw's lair could have beneficial effects. Crimson read and trained from a Manual of Upright Men (Bell of Climbing), and Ryndall read and trained from a Exercise Schedule for Bodily Health.

Nostradamus is still with Uskar as we begin. Nostradamus' divination suggests that we may find fame underground, but we disregard his prophecy. We set out for Thornycroft (to check out the story my siblings were told, that Releya has left the grove). A few days from home we meet a hand of Quaal, who tell us that five hands of cave elves are laying in ambush for us near the house.

I Commune with Dorshal:

  1. Has Releya recently left Thornycroft Grove? Yes.
  2. Should I be concerned abou ther leaving the grove? Perhaps.
  3. Are the kavalar in ambush around the house for us? Yes.
  4. Are they ambushing us for our actions in Ashertiri? Yes.
  5. Could they be mollified by the gift of the goods from the kavalar battlefield? Yes.
  6. Is Releya in the lands of the Quaal? No.
  7. Is Releya in danger? I don't think so.
  8. Is Molan in danger? Probably.
  9. Is Molan in the lands of the Quaal? Not entirely.
  10. Is there a message you would like me to deliver to your sister? You must not ask me that again, if you wish me to continue as your servant. (He loses his normal divine serenity, and the miracle ends.)

Ryndall and I approach the house with the swords, armor, and iron and fire from the battlefield. He makes nice with the Shanagates, and they accept the goods and make peace with us. The leader, Shan Shanagate, asks if he may meet our sister, and Ryndall agrees with some provisos. The others pack up the stuff and leave, with the warning that we should avoid Ashertiri for a year to let memories fade. Shan's teyn, Etienne High-Shanagate Villiers, quietly warns Ryndall that Shan may not be in complete control around her, though we will not be in danger.

We return to our siblings, then continue on to the house. Only the two lead kavalars are there, waiting for us. When Shan sees Missy, he falls to his knees and cries (in a language that only Uskar understands), "Fate! You are an arrow in my heart! Any other face but hers! Any other!" Etienne carries him to the aircar, then returns. Ryndall introduces Missy as "Missy, weilder of the Blade Cuisinart". Etienne leaves in awe.

In the house, we see that everything is in order, with only Mom's adventuring gear missing. I start a fire in the fireplace, and we settle in. It is the fifth week of winter, one week before Midwinter's day.
