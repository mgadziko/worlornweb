Adventure record - April 8, 1998

Ryndall notices that we are being followed by evil elf priestesses as we enter an alley in the Maze (bad part of town). In this section of town, we cannot expect any aid from the city guard. I invoke a miracle to obscure us from them by a mist, and we stay still hoping they will pass us by. They invoke an evil miracle to dissipate the mist, revealing us. Missy casts a magical mist of her own, and we move deeper into the Maze. We find the Dragon Piss Inn. I give a beggar a copper as we enter.

We get a table, and wait for Crimson to arrive. Several fights break out, and a group of Shanagate kavalars attempts to goad us, but we keep to ourselves.

When we decide that Crimson isn't coming tonight, we get up to leave. The kavs get up and block the door. Nightingale goads them, and combat begins.

In the combat, one of the kavs tips over Uskar's disk, dumping all our gear onto the floor. Nightingale strikes one with the Oaken Sword, turning him into a tree! Missy is wounded, as in Ryndall. Missy slays two in a single swing! Some hobbits grab some of our gear from the overturned disk, then scurry out of the bar chased closely by Uskar.

Ryndall shouts insults at the kavalars, and wounds one of them. Missy wounds another, who wounds her back. Missy then slays him with a second blow, narrowly missing more looting hobbits. One of Nightingale's opponents casts a spell upon himself, and when Nightingale hits him both fall. Uskar zaps one of the kavs with his wand, and the kav zaps him back (but Uskar is protected by an amulet). Ryndall is wounded again.

One of Ryndall's remaining opponents wounds him again. The downed kav strikes at a hobbit looting Nightingale's Oaken Sword. I race over to Nightingale. He's not dead, but unconscious, struck from behind with a mace by the looting hobbit. Ryndall slays another kav. Uskar zaps the same kav, but he now also has a magic missile absorption device ready.

Missy joins Ryndall's melee, and slays one. Ryndall wounds his opponent. Uskar sees the fleeing group of looting hobbits, one of whom has the Oaken Sword. Before he can act, an invisible hobbit is on his back with a knife at his neck. The kav strkes me and says he'll kill me. I reply "You should make your peace with your god."

He strikes me again. I say "Is that the worst you can do?" Ryndall slays one of the remaining two kavs. Missy slays the one attacking me, but his magic also slays her.

The hobbit forces Uskar to drop his staff, but Uskar slams backward into the tree, knocking the hobbit off. The other hobbits scatter. Uskar casts a spell which puts two of them to sleep, and fortunately one of them has the Oaken Sword, so he retrieves it.

Uskar retrieves as much of our gear as he can from the hobbits and brings it back inside. Some Ironjade kavs assist, after Ryndall speaks the correct words. One is a priest, and offers to restore Missy's life in exchange for the Shanagates' gear. While this occurs, Ryndall converses with one of the Ironjades, who helps him improve his kavalar insults. After the priest's ritual, Missy is restored to life! I grasp her and cry, "Stop trying to leave me!"

The Ironjade priest and I cure the wounded. Nightingale has a concussion, and should not join in combat for at least a moon.

The Ironjades offer to travel with us out of the city, and we agree. As we leave the bar, the Ironjades touch all of us, the priest casts a spell and the city fades out out around us.
