Adventure record - December 23, 1997

Missy "borrows" Uskar's magic crystal to scry for the Braith with the stolen midnight sword (Dorshal's sword). She sees him striking someone from behind, stealing a small item then running away. As she continues watching, he battles and slays again, then continues running through (city/town?) streets. He goes into a building with a sign of a "breathing bull" and drinks.

Missy writes some notes into Uskar's book. We continue toward Khan's Fort. 3 weeks travel from burial mound to Khan's Fort. Along the way, we meet a hand of black bunnies who wave at us but don't attack. We come to farm lands by the river. There are boats and barges by the castle.

We approach the castle. There are mounted soldiers with bronze armor. Miracle: Know customs on a mounted warrior; "spirit of Khansman"; I'm nearly overwhelmed by foreign knowledge (almost possessed), siblings are concerned at personality shift. We approach and enter the huge keep, with Khannish knowledge of customs We go to an inn, meet Charlie the hobbit ("locksmith" in a merchant train), get a room Leave Fernan and Uskar in the room, the rest of us rejoin Charlie who agrees to translate for us. We cross the street to the tavern and get dinner; some taal kavs buy us drinks.
