troy, awake!
theseus' lament		 







 
Her light shineth over the chaos,
Her light exists within the void, 
Her light illuminates the secrets,
Casting shadows over muses from ancient times.
 
-----------
 
Awaken great world for they future awaits,
No longer the strife of golds and archtypes,
Wild was the magic that destroyed the land,
All is anew within this awakened land.
 
A god named Troy Ounces must walk again,
for the gifts of wild magic must be given again,
he rules the land, I rule the air, and water we share,
Awaken and remember the heart, quill, and throne --
and a crown, ring, and scepter from that place we called home.
 
Rejoice again for all is anew,
the land's heart shall beat a new ancient tune,
ripped from your hands the powers you had,
but history is not forgotten so be ever so glad.
 
Awaken great Worlorn, your pattern's redone, 
I have sent my Arch Angel to oversee what I've begun,
Turf and power might be awarded some day,
If adventurers true to this world prove their worthiness  
in the night and the day.
--------------
 
Her light shineth over the chaos,
Her light exists within the void, 
Her light illuminates the secrets,
Casting shadows over muses from ancient times.
--
Theseus 020627
