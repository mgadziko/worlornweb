StarLight Armor of Shal Omnis

The Clerics (StarBringers) and Paladins (StarKnights) of Shal Omnis are easily identified, as they are dressed in the armor of the stars.  StarLight Armor is made of a ultra-strong mesh material that to the untrained eye, might resemble silk.  Star Armor is all-white, from head to toe, and produces a noticable aura at night -- which actually has a negative effect to Armor Class.

StarBringers(Clerics) wear a robe, secured with a utility belt around their mid-section.  StarKnights (Paladins) wear knee-high boots, pants, tunic-shirt, utilty belt, and cape.  The Klawefen can be found secured across the back in both cases. Star Armor, is the only authorized armor for StarBringers and StarKnights. The special feature of the armor allows wearers to travel on the astral plane (note: it does not transport member to the plane, merely acts as a "spacesuit" while they are there).

StarArmor (Statistics)

AC: 4 (AC6 in Starlight)
WT: Normal Cloth Weights
Resist Cold / Resist Fire
Special: Astral Plane Protection
