Main Temple of Shal Omnis

Location: Imperial Enseljos
Purpose: Place of Worship and Help for Followers of Shal Omnis
Description: The main temple of Shal Omnis is one of the more prominent structures on the Imperial Enseljos skyline. The catherdral towers resemble two upright dragons, wings stretched skyward, peering upward towards the stars. The eyes of the dragons glow brightly in the night sky, sending 40,000 candlewatt of light upward into the night sky.

Upon entry into the temple, one might believe that they are actually walking amongst the stars.  The floor, walls, and ceiling are pitch black with twinkling stars intermingled throughout the darkness, providing the light to guide the way. The eerie three-dimensional effect of the room can be unsettling to those whole have never walked amongst the stars. A "floating" greeting desk is always occupied so that visitors may seek the blessing and knowledge of Shal Omnis at any time of the day. Prayer and meditation rooms are available to those who wish them, as is temporary lodging for believers.

The temple sponsors many social activities to promote harmony and well being of all the citizens of Imperial Enseljos. If you are a traveller in search of help, you can find it within the Temple of Shal Omnis.
