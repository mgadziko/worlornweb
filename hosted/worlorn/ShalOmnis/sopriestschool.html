StarBringer School

Location: Imperial Enseljos
Purpose: Training of Priests serving Shal Omnis
Description: Domed building offset from the Main Temple in Imperial Enseljos. Entry to the school must be made by passing through the Main Temple. Priest candidates will live in this building for the duration of their training, communing between the Main Temple and the School for their instruction.
