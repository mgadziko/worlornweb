awaken hektor
you can’t win them all







Hektor is in a darkened room, bigger than he has a right to be in. A big room, all blacker than night, and cool, like a cave underground. Echoes and monk senses place the room at 50 feet across, much larger than any life hutch. Hektor’s not near any of the walls. He lifts his arm and finds the ceiling outside casual reach.
A bead of blue light appears, probably would have surprised a normal man. it forms a glowing humanoid. It is Troy Ounces, dressed alla-in-White. He’s sitting full lotus in the exact center ... of the Chamber of the Pattern, but it’s not at all right. There’s no magic within the stonework, no pull or glow from the inlaid silver and lapis making up the physical Pattern.
Troy looks up from his clasped hands, and when He meets Hektor’s eye, Hektor is there next to him with a thought.
Hektor are you still mine? asks his god. Into Hektor’s stunned silence, He continues.
They have washed Pattern clean. I need mortal blood of my own veins to redraw Troad in its power over shadow. Will you help us? Troy Ounces rises and lifts His hands to pass over Hektor’s bowed head, blessing him.
We must be Dust now, Hektor, says god-Troy. Do you understand?
Hektor awakens feeling more refreshed than he has in years.
