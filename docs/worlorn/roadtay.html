Ewnay Roadtay
hetay andslay ofyay Anngrisnirtay








Anngrisnirtay reatedcay Ewnay Roadtay otay eflectray hetay Atternpay ofyay Workinday Arimenbay. Oweverhay, Anngrisnirtay addedyay eightyay eilsvay otay Workinday'say wentytay-eilvay Atternpay, andyay hesetay additionalyay eilsvay ustainedsay Ewnay Roadtay hroughtay hetay endyay ofyay hetay Yclecay ofyay Reamingday.
Hetay eographygay ofyay hetay roadtay tartssay ithway hetay ontinentcay ofyay Artroxialay. Aterseaway ortspay existyay onyay allyay oastlinescay, otablynay Ortpay Uamesay andyay Ewnay Ostobletnay. Hetay Isleyay ofyay Leepsay ancay ebay oundfay onyay oneyay Artroxianlay inlandyay easay, roperlypay alledcay hetay Akelay ofyay Reamsday.







Anngrisnirtay
howay'say inyay hargecay erehay








Anngrisnirtay isyay engenway alemay outhyay inyay ishay atelay eenstay/earlyyay wentiestay. Ehay ayay tudentsay ofyay hetay onzebay emptyhandyay ightingfay aysway, 'illerkay-ayay' ojoday, andyay ashay omesay killsay asyay ayay izardway.

Orderyay
omnipresentyay archetypeyay








Hetay Orderyay ofyay hetay Orlornway Ystemsay inyay reviouspay yclescay asway ayay eflectionray ofyay Workinday'say Atternpay. Ithway Almitrayay'say Luxingfay ofyay hattay Atternpay, Orlornway ecamebay ayay easay ofyay Haoscay, rokenbay onlyyay ybay hetay illway ofyay ieldersway ustainedsay hroughtay earlynay hirtytay Oldgay Attributesyay.
Ithway hetay aidyay ofyay Hetay Ighhay Oneyay, Anngrisnirtay isyay raduallygay ringingbay hetay orderyay ofyay hetay Atternpay ackbay hroughouttay Orlornway.








hetay Akermay ofyay Ulesray
oldengay attributeyay ocusfay ofyay hetay roadtay








Hetay Akermay ofyay Ulesray isyay ayay endantpay onyay anyay unbrokenyay haincay arvedcay romfay ayay inglesay emeraldyay. Ityay isyay hetay enterpiececay ofyay hetay rimepay riotay ofyay Rojantay artifactsyay: Hetay Ringerbay ofyay Orderyay, hetay Akermay ofyay Ulesray, andyay Hetay Eweljay ofyay Udgementjay. Ithway hetay akermay, Roytay asway ableyay otay eray-arrangeyay hetay ealityray hichway hetay ringerbay ormedfay outyay ofyay Haoscay.
ieldingway hetay akermay isyay angerousday, asyay ityay rawsday romfay hetay ifelay orcefay andyay reamsday ofyay hetay earerbay, egardlessray ofyay hattay entityyay'say intentyay. Histay rainday isyay requentlyfay erceivedpay asyay ayay lowingsay ofyay imetay, allowingyay hetay earerbay otay eactray asterfay hantay hetay environmentyay ouldway ormallynay ermitpay. Actuallyyay lacingpay hetay haincay aroundyay oneyay'say ecknay ausescay onnay-rojanstay otay ieday, andyay earsbay ownday ayay rojantay ightilymay, asyay ifyay hetay eightway ofyay ayay orldway asway onyay ishay oryay erhay houlderssay. Hileway hetay haincay isyay ornway aroundyay oneyay'say ecknay, hetay akermay ausescay allyay upernaturalsay attacksyay onyay itsyay ielderway otay ebay efuddledbay otay eflectray uponyay hetay attackeryay evenfoldsay, egardlessray.
Hetay Akermay ofyay Ulesray isyay ayay okentay rantedgay ybay hetay Emeraldyay Tonesay inyay hetay Ourtscay ofyay Haoscay. Hroughtay histay emgay, Workinday asway ableyay otay ausecay ishay illway otay ebay eflectedray inyay ealityray, hustay reatingcay Roadtay.

hetay ighhay oneyay onyay Hetay Akermay ofyay Ulesray:
hetay akermay ofyay ulesray rovidespay, upportssay, andyay ontrolscay inenay-enthstay ofyay allyay anifestationsmay ofyay orderyay inyay orlornway, includingyay utbay otnay imitedlay otay reationcay, arbitrationyay, andyay enforcementyay ofyay anyyay ompactcay(say) etweenbay orlornway urftay. hetay awslay ofyay loodbay, ustday, illykinsbay andyay iresiastay areyay upheldyay andyay odifiedmay overyay imetay ithway hetay himway ofyay hetay ielderway ofyay hetay akermay ofyay ulesray.







Engenway
lessedbay eoplepay ofyay hetay Roadtay








Hileway hetay raditionaltay loodlinebay ofyay Roytay isyay tillsay otentpay inyay hetay aeriefay andyay reygay elfaryay, hetay ortalmay aceray ponsoredsay ybay hetay Roadtay isyay hetay Engenway eoplepay, ayay egroidnay aceray ofyay umanshay. Engenway areyay ayay reamingday aceray, andyay ighlyhay-ronepay otay sionicpay abilityyay.
Engenway ocietysay isyay ypicallytay hattay ofyay ovencay ruidsday, ithway hosetay otnay artpay ofyay hetay eepwoodsday ocietysay cornedsay asyay 'owancay' olkfay. Engenway howay avehay actuallyyay ledgedpay otay ayay ovencay irclecay onway'tay evenyay peaksay otay owancay acesray.
Hetay ighesthay opulationpay ofyay Engenway eoplepay inyay Artroxialay isyay inyay hetay Estway, hereway Roytay Ouncesyay isyay aidsay otay avehay ettledsay ithway ishay istersay hileway tudyingsay Orlornway inyay hetay irstfay earsyay.
