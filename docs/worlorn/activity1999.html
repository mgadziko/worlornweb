Most inspired activity in 1999 dealt with a group of five elf siblings, AKA lios elfar or “elves of light,“ who left their isolated home together to travel the world. They discovered a number of lios elfar clans who worshipped a deity of the same name as their father, travelled to all the clans but one, and made friends in each clan.
In the course of their travels, the siblings encountered a hermit-queen, founder of the enclave called the Swan. Queen Laurien introduced the elves to some friendly city folk, but was slain. However, the elven siblings were able to bring the Queen to her people again. Much interaction dealt with the city friends and their troubles before the queen was returned to crossing-in-the-mist with the elven siblings.
A homecoming song was composed by a player with a minstrel character. Unfortunately, the social dynamic of the gaming group changed abruptly, and this group stopped playing just before Laurien’s homecoming was performed.
