a dream for derrick
exhile becomes servant







Derrick is on his back in a dark room. the stone is cold under his back. after a moment of dread, he realizes he's back in the chamber within the octagonal building, where he awoke alone once before. Derrick pulls himself into the outer chamber, which lights up when his feet hit the floor.

like last time, there is a chamber with a black door on one end, and his crypt on the other. unlike the first time Derrick was in the room, he is not alone. there is a hairless monkey or butt-ugly boy in the room with Derrick. the child stands back, as far from Derrick as the room permits. the child is less than three feet tall, with huge ears. except for the metal Derrick carries, the creature's gear appears identical to Derrick's; however, each of Derrick's metal items appears in the child's gear as stone, wood, or bone.

the child„speaks?„without moving its lips. "I sorry," he says. "My fault."

with the thought, Derrick feels a wave of sorrow, feels it in his heart and soul as if it was his own pain, his own shortcomings, his own failures. Derrick closes his eyes and put hands over his ears, staggers against the wall and falls to one knee, but the depths of sorrow do not end. the mindpain is like a blinding flash„hot pink light showing through eyes held tightly shut.

"i _sorry_. i so sorry," says the child.

the apology is the only gentle thing left in Derrick's mind. waves of regret continue like a stormy ocean pounding at a lighthouse rock, driving his awake, awake, away.

Derrick lies with eyes shut for many minutes, remembering all his shortcomings as if it was judgement day. all his regrets, those forgotten and those never to be forgotten, they all come back to Derrick: every time he failed a mission, arrived late, forgot a birthday, or betrayed a trust. it is a long while before Derrick can strengthen himself to rise from his bed.

when Derrick sits up, there is an ugly child„or a hairless monkey„dressed almost exactly like Derrick, standing there in the room at Salt Peter's Estate. his dark eyes are sad, so very sad.
