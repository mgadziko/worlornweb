Adventure record - October 21, 1997

Attacked by kav elfar, Molan holds them off while the rest of us escape back into the caves (the dungeon below Dzur Mountain).

Light; attempting (failing) to blind an attacking kavalar
Cure Light Wounds; curing Ryndall
Augury; determine correct direction to go
Chamber with two great (evil lich?) female powers by a chessboard; they let us pass unmolested. (why?)
We stop to rest at a four-way intersection.

(I've been asleep for a while. My siblings fill me in -- they travelled with the humans to some kind of town. Rachel died and the humans held a funeral. Our hand travelled on to find a hand of Swans. At a cave at the base of Dzur Mountain we found them -- Mikhail, Molan, Fernan and others. Fernan is Quaal, but I didn't keep the whole story straight, as we're in constant danger in these caves.)
