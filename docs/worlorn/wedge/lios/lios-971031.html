Adventure record - October 31, 1997

Nolerak gifts me with Dorshal as my divine servant; I can use his bedroll as myfocus for gaining spells. I give up the "evil" hand; Molan gifts me with his.
* Augury; "Is it the will of Nolerak that I use Molan's hand to reveal the will of Nolerak?" Molan _feels_ the hand!

We plus Fernan Solar head for Diaspar in mid-autumn. On All-Hallows Eve, we meet the ancient Kestral Canaith; we discover Misty is possessed. I fail in an attempt to "turn" her. He sings "Beggar's Game" and she falls asleep. She was possessed by "Agate", spirit of an ancient (long-ago morganti'd) Kestral.
