Adventure record - May 5, 1998

It is the week of Midsummer. (I've been asleep a long time!)

After my wondrous vision of my Father, I awaken and begin to memorize the miracles granted me. During this time, my siblings and Mollan hold our position against various foes. By the time I come out of my reverie, we're out of the dungeon in a tree of the Quaal. I'm covered in the blood of one of Ryndall's victims. Crimson's nine-bladed sword was stolen during one battle, and Missy squandered a wish ring for a scroll of Find Familiar.

Mollan tells me of recent events. I call over the siblings, tell them of my vision, then Restore Mollan's level drains, and Heal his remaining head wound. For this and all other miracles, my holy symbol glows powerfully.

I Remove Curse on Uskar to restore him to full elf, removing the curse of being a half-elf/half-hobbit. He sees, through his spectacles Father with His hand on my shoulder, and goes into a religious ecstasy. I Cure Serious Wounds on Ryndall's hand, though opposed by some possessing force.

Uskar keeps the glasses on for 10 hours (while I meditate and pray) until he collapses. Missy picks his pocket and removes the glasses, but she sees nothing special. She offers them to Ryndall, who sees a fit human wearing a turban with a hand on Missy's shoulder. When he turns to me, he also sees Father, and also goes into religous ecstasy. He and Uskar compare notes about the turbaned one. They let Crimson wear the glasses, and he again sees the boy and hobbits sneaking around. They signal him to be coy and say nothing. He also sees some creature latched onto Ryndall's back. Then returns the glasses to Uskar who puts them away.

My leaf holy symbol acts as a High Alter for this divine intervention. I pray for Restoration for Uskar, and for for Dispel Evil for Ryndall. I cast the Restoration on Uskar. Then we spend three days, while I create Holy Water by capturing rain and dew in Dorshall's cloak. With that, I Dispel Evil on Ryndall, which dispels the foreign religious influence.

I lead a ceremony of thanksgiving, blessing and cleansing all us lios and gear. For the first time, Ryndall and Uskar take part with full enthusiasm!

Crimson's elf-slaying sword comes to Molan's attention. Molan, Crimson and Ryndall bargain and Ryndall returns the iron weapon to local coven. Molan agrees to help Crimson recover the nine-bladed sword. The coven make clear that they owe a favor to Ryndall, which he can claim at any coven fountain.
