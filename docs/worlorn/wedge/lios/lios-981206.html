Adventure record - December 6, 1998

summer (1 week before midsummer)

room in Wintercastle
  • Marsellus - away with Lorien near Isuame for a week for training and to explore alignment differences.
  • Ryndall & Crimson - trump to Keebler's Tree surprised by Rambo the ranger. He takes them to see "The Keeb". Keeb's not available, so Crimson will have to come back later. Crimson buys 16 boxes of cookies, but magic cookies only on sale at midsummer. Ryndall asks Rambo about ranger skills in Shando - they trade human/kav slaying arrows.
  • Tanri the harpist - sings & plays for room, board & tips. "Saracen" the flautist is another musician.
  • Derek - Larry the gnome leads Derek to the library of the "University of Seeming". Derek does some magic work for cash (and gets a gnome card).

I return with Lorien as Ryndall & Crimson return. We discuss plans. Ryndall removes Misty's water ring and lets it disappear, then puts his own fire ring on her finger instead. Crimson trumps us to Keebler.

3 days after midsummer

I wake up in a tree with Crimson's Uskar - the others are missing. It's daytime. The tree has two doors- one shows a forest (Uskar says Keebler) other shows swamp (Quaal). Uskar casts a spell and sees the others plus Lorien plus another male lios in combat with a coven and his monkeys. "We" defeat him, then they leave. Uskar puts on his spectacles and sees another female self with them. She calls herself Somana. She has bloody feet, and takes the fallen coven's mocassins.

Crimson hears Ryndall's voice outside the tree, at a long distance; the male lios leaves, since travelling with Ryndall et al is too dangerous. Somana wants to avoid the kavalar she's escaped from. Crimson says "Hey Misty, can you hear me?" and she _does_! They decide to return to us.

Someone named Annie (a friend of Crimson's) comes into our tree overnight, and converses with Crimson. They Trump out and we shut the doors of the tree at his request. Crimson is waiting outside the tree when redlight begins. The rest arrive at the end of redlight. I remove the curse on 3 kethi iron & fire, removing the green glow.

4 days after midsummer

Lorien, Somana, Tanri, Derek, Crimson & the siblings
Somana, Crimson, Ryndall & Uskar outside the tree, discussing how to place Somana using Crimson's Trump. A half eIf archer approaches and attacks Ryndall. Crimson uses a wind power to stop all arrows (including Misty's). Ryndall cuts the archer's bow in half! He attacks back with a dagger. Uskar magic missiles him. Lorien prostrates herself and says, "Lord Huron, forgive us, all we have is yours" in coven. I shout in lios "Stop fighting! Lorien says we should bow down and apologize!" Missy materializes her mist form in between Huron and Ryndall. She takes a hit and falls down. Lorien yells (in lios) "Drop your weapons- don't fight him!" Finally, combat stops. Lord Huron demands that one of us stay with him, wanting Uskar. Somana offers to go instead. "The rest of you may go" and we do. Somana gives Uskar an inequality sigil in gratitude for our saving her. As we leave, we find we're leaving an almost submerged stonehenge!

date?

I wake up. The party is already moving through a town. Lorien is leading, and Tintagel is here too. We arrive at Maja's house, surrounded by an iron fence with closed gates. As we consider, Crimson listens for Maja's voice on the wind. He doesn't hear her, but notices other wind powers in town, which notice him and send some creature toward him and us. A kavalar woman suddenly appears. She says she is owsla and wonders which of us is 10 bells. Crimson says he isn't 10 bells and the woman moves away.

A lackey opens the door for us. At the house, another lackey lets us into the mansion. saying that Maja is not home. I take Lorien aside, praying for "rapport" (which Dorshal grants) so Lorien can experience what I experienced when touched by Nolerak. Crimson goes in search of the kitchen. He finds a chef and gorges, then they play cards late into the night. Ryndall rests because this is a safe place.
