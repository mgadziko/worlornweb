Adventure record - March 25, 1998

As a group of courtly-dressed hobbits moves toward us across the floor, Missy slumps to the ground asleep. Ryndall catches her. One of the hobbits is Crimson.

Nightingale "helps" Missy over to a chair, out of the way, muttering about "too much drink". The hobbits introduce themselves to me and Uskar as Mallard L'Orange, Cabernet Sauvignon, Chateau Briand, Lonagrin, Tammy O'Shanter, and Crimson Grey. They call themselves the Kimdiss Land Grant Commission.

Crimson sets up to do a magic show for the crowd. During the show, I go over to Nightingale, and we arrange to have him (with help) take Missy back to the inn. Crimson's sleight of hand show is very successful. He makes items disappear then reappear in a flash of light, such as a handkerchief of the human king, and two feather tokens. He receives much applause and many gold coins (and a few platinum!) from the audience.

After the show, we elves (Ryndall, Uskar and I) are invited up to the Duke and his human guest. The guest names himself Carlos, Duke of the North of Elidor. He says that in his youth he saw Nolerak in his land, and invites us to come back with him to Elidor so his wife can see the vision (us) that he has seen. He asks our price, and Ryndall asks about Nightingale's hand. Carlos says that something may be possible. We'll meet again here tomorrow to arrange the travel.

Ryndall cleverly arranges to get the information to Crimson that we may be leaving tomorrow, and that we are staying at the inn named "The Garden of Delights". We are escorted back to the inn. Ryndall gifts the leader, Humus, with a feather token. We fill Nightingale in on what happened.

Meanwhile, Carlos gets Crimson alone in a room known as "The Sunlight Chamber" (because of its skylight) to get his handerchief back. Carlos asks whether Crimson is a Time Bandit and is after him. He tells Crimson that he is a time traveller, and offers to bring Crimson with him along with the elves to Elidor, and teach him more about time travel.

Aferwards, Crimson goes back with some mysterious hobbit cronies and Tamara, and hands over two (stolen) feather tokens to the local crimelord called "Captain Kirk Hornblower". Apparently, this wins some sort of bet, so he and Tamara receive many thousand gold coins. The crimelord turns the feather tokens over to some Phoenicians. Everyone is very impressed with Crimson.

Next day, we three (Ryndall, Uskar and I) go to the palace and are brought to the sun room. Carlos joins us in a few minutes, and asks if Crimson can come along. We agree and Crimson joins us. He says that he can take us with magical travel, back to a time before we were born, more than 1000 years ago..

He casts a spell. The suns turns into streaks in the sky. The room fades out, and we we find ourselves on an island in daytime. I invoke a miracle to determine that it is 1620 years in the past. We walk along the side of the (nearby) road south toward the seashore to a village. Ryndall recognizes this as the same place that the city will be. Carlos speaks with some tanned elves with hand motion. We enter a building with a golden disk on the floor. The room is guarded by females in armor, with spider holy symbols. We step on the disk with Carlos, and we are teleported to another similar, but smaller and unguarded, toom elsewhere. Carlos unlocks the door to the room, and welcomes us to his home. Uskar notes that the disk is not a magic item.

The main room is part of a stone castle. Soldiers report peace at the other three castles (south, east, and west). This is a much more rustic, frontier-style castle, than at Ashertiri. We are taken to a lady, working in a small flower garden in a courtyard. Carlos embraces her, then introduces her to us as Bella. She is very impressed, while Carlos enjoys her enjoyment.

Carlos takes Crimson off for some private conversation, while Ryndall, Uskar and I are given a tour of the grounds outside the castle by some guards. In the woods outside the castle Ryndall and I plant a Quaal feather token. I take a small branch I found on the ground. The castle is at the top of a cliff overlooking the ocean, and we take a trail down to the sea. Uskar takes some sand from the beach.

After a total of about three hours, we are all together again. Carlos returns us to our normal place and time, retracing the path we took to get to his castle. He brings a human priestess with him. He stays in the Duke's castle while we take her back to the inn. Crimson returns to his new friends.

We take the priestess to the room across the hall which we have not been using. Nightingale and I are alone with her as she performs a powerful miracle (using Chaotic Good language) to reattach Nightingale's hand to his arm. Carlos had told us that the priestess did not speak any language we knew, and she appeared to us as a human. But when we are alone with her, she changes form into a half-elf, and she addresses Nightingale as Molan, asking if he remembers her. Molan says he doesn't, and when I open the door to bring Ryndall in, she changes back into a human-appearing form before he can see. As Ryndall and Uskar return her to Carlos in the Duke's castle, Uskar with his spectacles sees that she appears to have wings! While they are gone, I bless Nightingale's hand in the name of Nolerak.
