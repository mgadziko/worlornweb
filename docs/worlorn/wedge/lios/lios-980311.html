Adventure record - March 11, 1998

It is two days since the attack. Tamara wakes up after additional healing. She wants to return to the cave to retrieve her weapon, which she says is a shape-changing weapon. She calls the ogres "Dr. Scofflaw and his Doomgivers". We explain that he was slain and burned. She says that his spirit would go to his familiar. We explain that it was also destroyed. She says that the familiar's "protector" would then take it and the spirit away. This seems to confirm Uskar's experience.

Uskar offloads the loot from the disk into one of the tree and we set off back to the cave. When we arrive, Molan "lights" his sword.

Augury: "Are there enemies of Nolerak in this cave?" The hand points off in a direction which I interpret to be end the far end of the cave. Molan says, "I felt my hand!"

The cave walls are all burnt. Uskar casts clairvoyance, and sees that the desk is damaged, but survived the fireball. The spell cannot view beyond the small stream. He casts another (for the other side of the stream) and sees the small evil creature, apparently burned but "alive", dragging the ends of the melted chain. The candle and candle-holder with the phrase "Light this when one servant is not enough" is on this side of the stream.

We enter the cave. At the intersection, Molan and Marsellus wait while the others go down the right branch. They find an unscathed bag of grain, which Tamara asks Ryndall to carry out. Uskar looks at it with the glasses, and it still looks like a bag of grain. We all retreat back out of the cave.

As we travel back to the tree, she speaks to the bag. Just as we get back, it responds, then turns into a silver mace. She puts the mace into her belt. She tells Crimson that it is a "slaver weapon", a legendary artifact. She also tells that she is gathering the ogre sweat (not spit after all) for a mage who will then heal her sister.

We prepare spells to go back to destroy the evil. I am unable to reach Dorshal, so I Sanctify my prayer area and Bless myself, then try again. Ryndall makes a new bowstring for Tamara. Tamara converses with Ryndall, Uskar, Crimson, and Missy. I am still not able to reach Dorshal, but prepare as best I can.

We head back to the cave. At the entrance I cast detect evil. We enter and Tamara shows us a hiding spot in the ceiling. Ryndall boosts Crimson up so he can hide and guard our back. Molan and I are in the lead with Missy and Uskar next with Ryndall in the back. Uskar casts protection from evil on Tamara and himself. We walk down the corridor. Uskar says that he sees the creature, then blasts it with his kavalar wand. It appears not to be affected.

We pass through the illusionary wall and see the creature. Missy and Tamara run away in fear of it. I cast protection from evil while Molan uses his sword to cause a bright light.

I cross the stream a distance from the creature, while Molan and Ryndall engage it. I begin the Sanctify ritual. The creature becomes invisible but Molan and Ryndall strike it anyway, and Uskar again shoots his kav wand (but again with no apparent effect). Uskar starts telling the others where the invisible creature is, greatly aiding their combat. Within a few minutes they defeat it, and it becomes visible. Ryndall cuts its head off. While I finish the ritual, Molan and Ryndall destroy and desecrate the candle and candle holder. Using my holy symbol, I attempt to Damn it but this has no apparent effect. (It must be more powerfully evil than I am capable of affecting, or I must be doing it wrong.)

Ryndall and Molan destroy the gold chain. To delay its regeneration, we chop it into small pieces and feed them to a wolf (controlled by one of Ryndall's magic rings). We estimate that this will keep it harmless for years, giving us time to devise a more permanent solution. We leave the cave.

Over the next few days, I bless and cleanse everyone, and Ryndall finds a local Quaal hand and asks that the cave be guarded to prevent accidents.
