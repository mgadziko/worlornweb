Adventure record - June 9 1998

It is the fifth week of winter, one week before Midwinter's day. Uskar is asleep.

We leave Nostradamus to care for Uskar while we're gone. The rest of us (Ryndall, Missy, myself and Crimson) travel twelve days to Hobbiton, so Crimson can find someone to help him find something called "The Pattern". During the journey (on Midwinter's Day), we come across a human encamped in the snow, wearing a yellow sash.

We arrive at the white picket fence. We come across a hobbit, all dressed in black, outside the fence. He and Crimson chat in hobbit language for a while. The one in black would like us to take him with us when we enter Hobbiton. Crimson does seem a bit uncomfortable with him. He says that he's called "Slimy" in New Nostoblet, but that it's a bum rap. He walks with us a day till we get to the gate in the fence. There are two gatekeepers inside the gate, which is shut. The gatekeepers are asleep.

Crimson wakes them up and asks to come inside. They ask him if he's scary, and he says "No". They ask if we elves are scary, and he says "No". They say to the hobbit in black "Nice duds!". We all walk in, then the hobbit in black vanishes, though we can still see some of his footprints in the snow. We continue on toward town.

Along the way, we help a farmer get his pony-drawn cart unstuck from a snowdrift. He then gives us a ride in his cart. (Crimson takes the reins, as the farmer keeps falling asleep and driving the cart into a snowdrift.) In a day, we reach some sort of castle, very large, which Crimson identifies as "Bardhold". From the outside it appears a very imposing keep, but it is really a hollow shell holding eight wooden-contructed inns, and some miscellaneous shops. It is inhabited mostly by human bards (surprise!) Crimson arranges for overnight lodging. In the room, he finds a small bag with some coins and a cryptic note written in hobbit.

Canaith Kestral joins us! He tells us his name is Kirak, and that "Canaith" is a title. We introduce him to Missy, who is awake to meet him this time. We tell him of our adventures since we left him. Our stories take two days. He helps explicate some of the stories. For example, it was Dark Agnes whose possession he dispelled from Missy, who was Shan Shanagate's love, and Jil Sander his worst enemy. If he knew that Missy had held a remnant of the spirit of his love, then finds that she now wears Jil's face, that could explain Shan's reaction on meeting Missy. He also advises us to seek Morgan the Faerie if we are ever on Elidor again -- she is the only mortal who he believes can tell just exactly who Missy is now. He advises us that the note could help prevent possession, and so perhaps Uskar should hold it.

Crimson shows his Deck of Trump to Kirak, who is very wary. Kirak seems to know a little about what he calls "Kin", and desires never to come to their attention. Crimson opens the box of cards, and is bitten by a small poisonous spider. Ryndall kills the spider, and I invoke a miracle to cure him of the wound. Canaith speculates that "Eve" (the Kin who gave Crimson the box) actually meant the spider as a gift, in that having survived its poison he now may be immune to it in the future. Some gift!

Tomorrow we will continue on toward Hobbiton proper, so that Crimson may seek an audience with some official called "The Chamberlain". It is the seventh week of winter.
