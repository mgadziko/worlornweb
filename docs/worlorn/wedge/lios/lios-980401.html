Adventure record - April 1, 1998

It is five weeks into winter.

Crimson arranges to train others and receiving training for six weeks.

Meanwhile, in the inn a jester enters and puts all the guards to sleep. He sings and cavorts outside our door. When he knocks on our door, Uskar puts him to sleep. Nightingale and Ryndall go wake up all the guards.

Crimson joins us. The guards tie him (the jester) up, then wake him up so Uskar can question him. Ryndall and Crimson take him into one of the rooms. In the move he drops his hat and jester wand. I pick it up and begin laughing, maniacally and unstoppably, until they untie the jester and he takes back the wand and touches me with it.

The jester calls himself "Mac" and says that he is the "good news", here to warn us of the "bad news". The bad news, he says, is that someone named "Mort" will be coming to search for us, to gain information about some queen or witch. (I guess that this is the priestess who reattached Nightingale's hand.) Mac says that he had a premonition of death or serious harm for our guards if we stay in this inn.

At one point in the conversation, Ryndall begins to daydream. Mac begins making marionette motions, and Ryndall begins to dance, caper, and laugh. We are alarmed, but he says that unlike others who do this, he is "a good guy" and is only playing. Ryndall quickly recovers. (Mac says that there are 2**4 such people. Chrysofar is one, he says, whom we met in Quaal last year.)

We decide to heed Mac's warning, and prepare to leave town. The inn's proprietor is renaming the place the "Queen's Head", in honor of Missy's visit. On the way out, we return the loot from Dr. Scofflaw's lair to the authorities so it can be returned to it's rightful owners. At another shop we acquire some containers of acid (after Ryndall does a little haggling on price). The proprietress, Elissa, really desires Uskar's red glasses. Afterwards, Crimson takes his leave to take up his training activities. We arrange to meet on Zeepsday at the Dragon Piss Inn. During our absence, Crimson arranges for a Gnome Bank account for his ill-gotten booty.

We travel two weeks to Scofflaw's lair, dissolve the remains, then travel two weeks back to Ashertiri. During the return journey, Uskar tries to read one of the magic palimpsests. It is cursed and takes away 40,000 ep, leaving him at 3rd level. Ouch! He burns it to keep anyone else from being damaged.

On our return it is the final week of winter. Nightingale helps Missy prepare a disguise. We go to meet Crimson on Zeepsday. As we move through town, Ryndall and I detect that we are being followed by a swarthy human. The human warns us that assassins are looking for us, one of whom is named Ryld. He warns that it's Nightingale's damaged boot that they are tracking by. We detour into a shoemaker's to get new boots for Nightingale. To get the best price, he names himself "Molan Nightingale", which impresses the proprietor no end. Then we continue toward the Dragon Piss Inn.
