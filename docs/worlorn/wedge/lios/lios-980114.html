Adventure record - January 14, 1998

We were suddenly surrounded by Brein Mark lios who are here for their sword. Their leader is Shar Asteril. He advises us to hide while his troop attacks the Braith. We "retire within the lands of the Quaal". Missy (with her crystal) and Uskar (with his magic) view the battle: green glowing Braith are losing to the Brein, except for the lead Braith with the special sword.

While praying for spells, Dorshal appears to me in a vision. He brings me a leather scroll, and indicates that I should read the questions written on it with amber sap ink.

  1. "Was something preventing you from coming to me that time?" Dorshal answers "Yes"
  2. "Did it have anything to do with the swan lady?" Yes
  3. "Did she do it on purpose?" No
  4. "Does she regret it?" Yes
  5. "Is she going to give me the rest of this commune as payment for the inconvenience?" Yes
Then I am allowed to ask nine more questions of my own:
  1. "Is it the will of Nolerak that I should not cast Augury until the hand has released the ring?" No
  2. "Are the Black Swans mistaken in believing that Nolerak is not the same god they worship?" No
  3. "Do the Black Swans offend Nolerak?" I don't know
  4. "Did the White Swans offend Nolerak?" I don't know
  5. "Are the green-glowing Braith a problem that we should deal with?" No
  6. "Will a Remove Curse miracle successfully remove the green stain from Ryndall's palm?" Yes
  7. "Are you happy?" Yes
  8. "Would we be welcome in the Forest within a Forest?" I don't know
  9. "Was the faerie telling the truth when he said he could arrange safe passage from Wunib to the Keebler's Tree?" Yes
I awake with Remove Curse granted, which I immediately cast upon Ryndall. I fill in the others about my vision, and Fernan chastises me for not asking about the sword.

At redlight, we exit and find Shar Asteril lying wounded. I Cure Light Wounds on him. We leave Uskar and Crimson with him and seek other wounded toward the south. We come across the remains of a battle. A red headed woman is searching the bodies. I aid two others with Cure Light Wounds. These are _very_ experienced lios, all gravely wounded. Missy and I leave Ryndall and Fernan to guard the wounded while we go back for the disk. We meet Uskar coming this way, and we three return to the tree. Missy takes the disk back to get the wounded then Uskar and I look for more wounded to the north. We reach the main battle site. Uskar detects evil -- the three green-glowing kav corpses _are_ evil. At this site, all survivors have been slain and all bodies have been looted. We return to the tree and find our siblings and the other wounded.

Uskar uses his crytal and sees "one arm" in a skyskoot moving away from us, still with the sword.

Missy stays with the wounded while Ryndall, Fernan, Uskar, Crimson and I take care of the dead by taking them to the river (to return them to the sea). While working (with some looting), Crimson is attacked by scavenging wolves. He and Fernan drive them off. After five days of work, we have cleansed the three battle sites of lios dead. I ask Nolerak's blessing to cleanse all of us who handled the dead.
