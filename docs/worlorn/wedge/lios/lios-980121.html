Adventure record - January 21, 1998

After 3 weeks of recuperation, the four surviving Brein part from us, and set off to follow the Braith.

Augury: "In which direction is it the will of Nolerak that we travel?" The hand glows and points northwest (downriver), revealing the "shortest path to that which Nolerak seeks". As we travel, we are ambushed by orcs, who drop nets on us. This also captures an invisible companion, which quickly cuts itself free.

The orcs carry us an hour or so north to their village. They set up iron pots and fill them with water. Missy tries to cast a spell on their (female) shaman. They kick her and the shaman approaches to kill her. Crimson throws a dagger at her which knocks the weapon from her hand. Missy magic missiles her. Fernan casts light at her eyes to blind her. Uskar moves the disk into her, knocking her down. I show the hand and shout, "I wish for the will of Nolerak to be manifest now!"

There is a buzzing sound, and all orcs close by are cut to ribbons and we are cut free of our nets, wounding us. Missy credits Fernan, but a perfect female elvish voice says "It wasn't Fernan". We flee, and as we run, our invisible companion slays many more of our orcish pursuers.

Uskar casts a fireball at some orcs to one side. (I have a vague dream that he cast some other spell which was not successful, then he wished that he had cast fireball instead.) I fail in an attempt to entangle a group to the other side, so Missy, Fernan, Ryndall, and Crimson must attack them in melee, slaying most of them. Missy is severely wounded, so I interpose myself between her and her attackers. I wish the remaining orcs would run away and they do (upon which they are chopped up by our invisible companion).

We get back to our starting point (the tree) and retire within the lands of the Quaal, much the worse for wear.
