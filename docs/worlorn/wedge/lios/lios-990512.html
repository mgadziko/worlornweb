Adventure record - May 12, 1999

My dreams of Marsellus have come to an abrupt halt, as he seems to have fallen deeply asleep for some time. The last few scattered snatches of him in my dreams were very unpleasant, tied up and clutched close by a sweaty, smelly, flying ogre, flying high and cold across unfamiliar lands toward an unknown fate.
