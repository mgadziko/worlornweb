Adventure record - March 5, 1998

I tend Crimson and Tamara's wounds, while Uskar loots the ogre mage's body. Tamara tells Crimson that she was living with the ogres, to gain material components (ogre spit) for some mage, then one day they attacked her. She offers to share the ogres' treasure with us, and will pay us if we will take her to a human place named "Ashertiri" (and suggests to Crimson that she can introduce him to her organization there).

I set up a Quaal tree, and take the wounded (Crimson and Tamara) inside, along with Missy. Molan, Ryndall and Uskar stay in another.

The next morning, we burn the ogres on a pyre, with a miracle for long-lasting fire. A bit later, a group of Quaal come across us. They offer us winter cloaks (two each), and Molan accepts for us (not correcting their mistaking him as our leader). They complement Molan for his "student's" song, by which they may be referring to his fighting skill. One of them is Moth, sister to Uskar's teacher Void. She tells that Void spent the previous winter at the Salvador Deli.

I cure Crimson and Tamara to the best of my ability. The healers of Moth's hand lend their aid as well. Then they depart. I remain in the tree with the wounded while Ryndall, Molan, Missy and Uskar go to check out the ogre's lair.

At the entrance, Missy sets off a fire trap. All her hair and clothes are burned. Molan lends her his cloak while Ryndall and Uskar laugh. They all move into the cave. Missy finds Tamara's clothes, and dresses. She also finds Tamara's (four full and one empty) vials. The four ogres have piles of treasures. Missy finds some kind of magical secret wall, and Uskar can see through it with his glasses.

On the other side is some sort of laboratory, with bubbling potions and a very large bat (almost hobbit sized!), chained and sitting on a perch. Uskar detects that the creature is vilely and grossly evil, even more evil than the creature in the Salvador Deli. He zaps it many times, until it falls limply to the floor. Then they all pass the imaginary wall into the chamber. The room is split by a small water channel, with the ogre mage's bed, desk, and treasure on one side and the perch on the other. Missy sees that any two pieces of this treasure is worth the whole of the other four's.

They loot the place, taking wine, scrolls, jade spheres ("coven eyes", Molan calls them), tapestries, and precious metals and artifacts. Apparently these ogres had been waylaying merchant caravans on the nearby asphalt road. They move the loot outside the cave, then Uskar shoots the fireball down the cave. Uskar sees (after the fireball blast) a dark force that collects the burnt bat corpse and whooshes out the cave, but no one else sees this.

They return with the loot to the rest of the party.
