Adventure record - March 30, 1999

Late autumn; Ryndall, Marsellus, Missy, Uskar, Crimson, and Derek in a tree with the sleeping Tanri. For-Derek, For-Tanri, and For-Crimson are here as well. We're far from the Enigmatic Grove now, in a forest near the base of some mountains. Outside the tree is Amar the ranger and a winged woman named Anael, whom my siblings met while I've been asleep. They say that Anael came here from a land far away, and that she had a vision of helping us.

We leave a note for the sleeping Tanri and head west toward Berolyn, about five days travel away. Amar takes his leave of us, to head toward Ironjade. We head southwest, with Anael carrying Crimson and For-Crimson to allow us to travel at our full pace, so the trip should only take us two days.

In the evening we encounter a pixie, who asks if Anael knows someone named Gisten. Anael says she doesn't. Later Richelieu's Son's Son happens across us again, and is disappointed to find that Tanri is not with us. He heads out in the direction we came from.

The next evening we meet some coven, who ask if we planted an oak in the Enigmatic Grove. When we confirm that we did, they are pleased and say we did well. They tell us that we (the deepwoods elves) are welcome in Shando as long as we like. At some point, the "For's" have vanished, but I don't know exactly when. The next morning, the coven share breakfast with us and Anael, though they do not seem to like Derek and Crimson.

Our last day's travel to Berolyn is across a swamp. Derek flies by magic, and Anael ferries the reset of us. It takes till dark to get everyone across. Berolyn is a town in the middle of a swamp, with building raised on stilts, and the streets are watercourses cleared of plants. Travel through town is accomplished by boat. At the center of town is a large fountain, which is the source of the water which flows in a river to the ocean at Port Suame, we're told.

We notice an odd occurrance: over the course of the day, we see washerwomen working at the fountain gradually transform into men! After redlight, the nightlife begins. Missy begins singing and the crowd joins in, which becomes a party! The inhabitants are mostly human, with a few lios, and a few hobbits. After a while, the bronzers appear and gently break up the party.

A Swan woman who introduces herself as Nony invites us to her place, which is a set of treehouses set into a baobab tree. Uskar notes that she has a lot of books. Nony is a sage, who has been specializing in collecting information about the faraway lands on the eastern edge of the continent.

It is two week's till winter.
