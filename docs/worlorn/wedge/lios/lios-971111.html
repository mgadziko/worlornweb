Adventure record - November 11, 1997

The darklings provide:
  • a golden elven sword (a "Laurethian Mark" sword; I place in Missy's gear)
  • a pair of spectacles (I place in Uskar's gear)
  • a ring with 3 swans (a "Laurethian Mark" ring; 3 wishes)
Augury; "Is it the will of Nolerak that I use this ring to restore to Ryndall the skills he lost to the undead?" Molan's hand clenches the ring; I surmise that it will release the ring when it _is_ Nolerak's will.

We continue toward Diaspar in winter, carrying messages for the Darkling's. In winter, Ryndall's skills are infallible -- a gift from the WinterMan to the Children of Releya. Fernan's father (the Quaal) was also a child of Nolerak and Releya. We stop at the Kimdiss village of Hillhaven; I provide blessed food and drink for a banquet. Midwinter we arrive at Thorp, a small human town near Diaspar. I again provide blessed food.

We reach Diaspar; I fear the civilization. We move through the outlying suburbs toward the Dome. Ryndall addresses two bronzers about his errand. Crimson Gray spends time with the cityfolk and has an interview with the Pope (and another withthe Ferret). Next day, the Pope comes out to the wood to meets us -- Ryndall returns the Cardinal's ring. Ryndall explains his need and some old man ("for a Child of Releya") touches him with mutual divine intervention: "Let it be known that the will of Nolerak and the will of Troy can be the same." Ryndall has a vision of Releya and Dorshall and is restored!
