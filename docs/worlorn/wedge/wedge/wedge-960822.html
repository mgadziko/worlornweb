Adventure record - August 22, 1996

diGriz runs an unrecorded adventure, ending at Firecastle.

1-25-4489 (45 Necrodemus). We want to go to High Asgar for Orbfest, which is on the 28th. diGriz begins at Firecastle, and travels through fire to a teleporter disk room (somewhere). The drow attack him (ineffectually). He goes into the wind, moves onto the pad, then jumps to High Asgar's teleporter disk. They're not friendly at that end, either, but he blows them down with wind, then goes out into the city (coming out of the wind).

He wanders around town for a while, going into a kavalar bar, then into a hobbit bar (where he gets into a dispute with the bartender).

Paris trump him, and Marlee tells him to "get his ass in gear". diGriz goes to the "drow tunnel" entrance outside High Asgar, where the Childean party is waiting. (A Childean named Avarros is the tunnel guard.) diGriz narrowly avoids falling under the Dark Horse's shadow, as he is outdoors at night and not in the wind!

Marlee goes with Maeglin and the Dark Childe party through the drow tunnels. One of the lowest rank Childeans, named Sennis Carsultyl, gets a temporary promotion to Maeglin's teyn (as Paris has to lead the Phoenecian party). Wedge, Marisse and Shan go with Paris and the Phoenecian party by ship.

The Childeans arrive first. Marlee does political maneuverings; the five regents are split two against two, with one undecided, for the choice of the new sixth regent (Kallion vs Chup); the Emporer is an underage figurehead.

Wedge and Marisse become intimate on the ship journey. The Phonecian fleet arrives, and Shan rejoins diGriz. Paris will become Pope, by bargaining with his ability to resurrect.

Wedge and Marisse "play the Palace" and get lots of money. Wedge's legen lore: a council of six kings built High Asgar to rule the continent. Each year, the elected a seventh as Emporer; if they were unanimous, the Empire would last another year. Wedge plays Stone Brother songs to the Dwarven royal party (King Constantine); this spreads the word that Wedge is a dwarf-friend. He tells stories of the Deathgod and the Stone Brothers.

On Orbfest (1-28), there is a big public loyalty-swearing ceremony. Wedge and Marisse avoid by staying on the ship; diGriz avoids by staying in his hotel. Marlee, Maeglin and Paris as political figures attend. Kallion is chosen over Chup as the new Regent, picked by Twaddlesham (the undecided Regent); Marlee recognizes Twaddlesham as a character from Novus Mundi!!

The boy Emporer, the son of Simon (the previous Emporer), appears on the balcony surprizing all but Kallion and Twaddlesham, who exchange a look and a smile. The Emporer is attended by a 14 year old girl, who moves as a monk.

Later in an indoor (but still "public") ceremony, all the Kings swear loyalty. Maeglin pledges the old pledge, to uphold Necrodemus and his Empire; he and Twaddlesham share a glance. The High One (another title for the Emporer) unexpectedly and personally announces Paris as Pope; apparently the young Emporer is trying to exert his authority over the Regents. Paris says that he'll bring people back now; the Emporer wants his father back.

Marlee does a Lohan know thoughts of all minds on the Emporer; he "recognizes" the Almitra-ness in her [Almitra is his grandmother] and makes her so (or Marlee does it involuntarily by Lohan power). Apparently, Marlee isn't Marlee, but rather "Sentient Deb" who's been masquerading as Marlee, but who can be any of Deb's characters.

We end our adventure. Afterwards, diGriz realizes that he's over his Stealthmaster magic item limit, and will have to jettison the excess or be punished. He's having a hard time deciding, though, as he likes all his stuff!
