Adventure record - August 31, 1996

Before the adventure begins, in the "between adventures" place, diGriz finds his roke staff and clasp, Almitra finds a Water Heart (and fashions it into a pendant), Wedge finds a jade skull "with his name on it" (and leaves it behind), and Cory finds some loot, picks it up, then disappears from diGriz' perceptions. Each of us exits by their black door.

1-30-4489 (45 Necrodemus) [as if such things mattered on Elidor]. At the crossroads on Elidor, diGriz, Almitra, Wedge, and Marisse start. Shan remains asleep. It's a grassy plain with scattered trees. One tree nearby and the ground around it is burnt and scorched. Nearby also is the cairn of stones. In the sky is a horsehead nebula. Almitra puts up a darkness to protect diGriz from the Dark Horse.

Wedge and Marisse open the chest. Inside is a 14 year old boy (Duncan, the Emporer) with his orb, scepter and crown. diGriz uses the chest for cover to go to the burnt tree, the Tree of Evocation. At the tree is an item for diGriz (from Cory) with proof against shadows and night. Also there are four large chests.

Wedge tells Marisse stories of the "grand old days" of the Empire, with Duncan as the influence reaction target. A mechanical "crab" contraption walks up the path and delivers a message to diGriz from the Watersea: "Good show old boy", then leaves. Duncan asks diGriz if he killed Duncan's father. diGriz waffles but essentially says "no".

We examine the four chests. One says "Diamond Stone", two say "Memory's Little Helper/Star of Preservation", and one says "Other booty". We move all but one chest (one of the "Memory's Little Helper"s) out of the burnt area away from the tree. While we stand away, diGriz opens the chest, and finds a gem-encrusted set of chainmail lying on a cube of living flesh. He picks up the chainmail.

Meanwhile, Wedge begins telling the histories of Mogan la Faye. Then out of the grass steps Saltpeter. Using time, he takes all of diGriz' material possessions, then gives them back. He appears to have some enmity toward diGriz.

Almitra opens the other "Helper" chest, puts on its chainmail shirt, then takes it off whereupon it disappears. We also open the "Other booty" chest; it contains:

  • Dagger of Ounces #20 -- diGriz takes this
  • Dagger of Ounces #31 -- Duncan takes this
  • Jester Trump
  • Trump Jester
  • an orb of sentients [no one takes this, but later it becomes clear that Saltpeter came back for it ]
  • Mirror of Troy Ounces
  • Troy's Eye of Emerald -- Wedge sets it up, but there are no Kin present to use it (as Duncan is not 1st level yet).
  • Maker of Rules

Saltpeter returns to Gorias (one of the four castles on Elidor). Almitra, Duncan, Wedge and Marisse follow on foot, heading east. diGriz and the sleeping Shan remain behind. After a four or five hour walk, we arrive at Gorias. Saltpeter is waiting with dinner.

Saltpeter gives Almitra a "present" for diGriz in a rosewood box, and a wish ring for her. She says "I wish that Duncan's father, my son, the Necrodemian Emporer Simon, were alive and well here with us now." Saltpeter puts his head down on the table and sighs "I am thrice-damned". Morgan la Faye comes down the stairs. Saltpeter teleports to her, then leads her back upstairs.

Threnody appears (in Dark Childe form), and Marisse falls asleep. He says to Almitra, "Ask of me anything else; this I cannot do." Almitra and Duncan refuse to retract the wish. He tells Duncan to wait a year and a day [till September 1, 1997!], then leaves. Marisee awakens and Saltpeter returns.

Duncan (after this divine intervention) wants to become a priest. Saltpeter offers to train him. He also tells Duncan that his scepter is a false one. Almitra goes up to Morgan; together they watch dragons fly by.

Back at the tree, the next morning (1-31) at sunrise, the tree bursts into a plasma flame, then goes out, unharmed. diGriz wants a branch; Brushfire the Incompetent comes up and tells diGriz to trade the tree a toe or finger for it if he wants to avoid the druid's enmity.

Almitra, Duncan, Wedge and Marisse return from the castle. Duncan tests a bit of time travel, and Saltpeter pops in long enough to retrieve his wish ring then leaves. diGriz searches and finds a druid, Elizabeth of Myada (the Elidor coven). She cuts off his left little toe then heals the damage. Working with the tree, she cuts off a branch then heals the damage, and gives it to diGriz.

Almitra gives diGriz the "present" from Saltpeter. She checks the box somehow and determines that it contains the Fire Sermon, plus (or as) a morganti weapon. diGriz declines to open it.

Almitra opens the emerald box containing the Maker of Rules (an emerald pendant). She puts it on, and transforms into a unicorn to prevent dying [as a chaotic character weilding a lawful artifact?]. She knows that she will need a cleric to cast a 6th or 7th level spell to keep her from dying, even if she takes it off.

We end our adventure by the tree (outside the burnt area).
