Adventure record - July 24, 1996

diGriz returns to Fountains, joins Wedge on 1-18-4489 (49 Necrodemus). We confirm that Threnody controls the Fountains as Turf.

Someone external tries to Gate a fire rat, diGriz asks Threnody to not allow it.

Threnody teaches Wedge the Words of recall to Flambeau and the Fountains, and gives him Tongues (1st Ed.) as the "Messenger of the Gods". Mort, an emissary from Yama, arrives and gives Wedge a St. Luces Baseball cap.

Marlee wakes up and joins. We all discuss ways to destroy Maeglin's Fire Sermon. Discussion: we need information on Turf balance; this may be more important that Sword balance. What does it mean to be a Roke mage? diGriz flexes his (mostly unused) Roke perceptions. The Paths of the Dead and the Fountains of Paradise are now Turf. Other possibilities are: Troad, Musquel, North Pole, Dolbadarn, Gold Road, District of Columbia, Knock, Elidor, Ogygia, Watersea...??

Wedge (inadvertantly) says "We're off to see the wizard" and teleports to Diaspar. It still stands, and is populated. He exits the city and returns to the Fountains via the Word.

Discussion indicates that Craig Donn ("Stonehenge") is an elemental power that may have some answers. diGriz, Marlee and Wedge travel (via Threnody's mirror) to near Stonehenge. We're ambushed by Lios with slaying arrows. diGriz takes us into the wind. We hear them talking about a slain comrade, so Wedge gives them his rod of wood elf resurrection.

At Craig Donn, we (still in the wind) assist some elves and human druids to kill a sleeth (a psionic monster). We show ourselves. Marlee and Wedge, as bards, are tolerated. diGriz, as an elemental, is very welcome. He joins their ritual, and perceives the Mother.

Marlee perceives in a mist the "shade" of Barrion, slain many years ago by a morganti weapon. She does her Lohan "do whatever it takes" to help bring him back.
