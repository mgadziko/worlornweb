Adventure record - July 31, 1996

diGriz, Marlee and Wedge, continuing at Craig Donn on 1-19-4489 (45 Necrodemus), still in the Druid ceremony.

Marlee hooks the Dark Horse to help in restoring Barrion. The sky becomes black, with no stars. The stars return when the Dark Horse comes to earth and approaches the stone circle.

diGriz' Roke perception tells him that Craig Donn is an "alter of witnessing".

The Dark Horse says that diGriz destroyed his sister and refuses help unless she is brought back first. That he communicates at all may be due to Marlee's "do anything". diGriz won't allow the circle's protection down, so the Dark Horse leaves.

Wedge notices (by its absence in the protected circle) that he feels the oppression when anyone dies. diGriz tries to "open a gate" on the Heelstone of Craig Donn, by striking it with his Roke staff. diGriz' staff takes root and becomes a tree, Donnerbaum, the Tree of Knowledge (a baby oak). Wedge is sent back to the Paths. diGriz gains the knowledge (not communication): "You must put me back in my proper place before you release me, or you may never touch earth again." He asks how to return it. Answer: "Any way you like." Druids want diGriz and Marlee to leave, but diGriz says he can't (afraid of the Dark Horse's vengeance?) Druids in procession all touch the tree.

[aside, Wedge in the Paths: are there any Roke mages in the Paths? There is a former Saltpeter, but Kevin Landwaster never came. Roke mages may "pass beyond" rather than die of old age. Wedge returns (outside the circle) via the Deathgod's mirror.]

Marlee touches the tree and asks how to bring Barrion back. Answer: "This is outside my knowledge. Iss Hogai has worked for others." She and diGriz wait for the Druids to complete their ceremony, releasing the protection of the circle. diGriz Transforms (as a Power) the tree into a staff. Wedge touches it and asks where it belongs. Answer: "My master Forelithe planted me in Karli."

Maeglin misses Marlee (the presence of the Dark Horse gives him a longing for the Abyss), and trumps us back to the Fountains. DiGriz has a secret talk with Paris. With Threnody's help, the tree is made into diGriz Roke staff, to put the need to return it at bay temporarily.

Consequences: if Marlee ends without accomplishing her "do anything" goal, then her "automatic" Lohan power will accomplish it, by any means necessary. For example, diGriz could be sacrificed to the Dark Horse to gain his favor and Wedge could be transformed into Eressea so she could cooperate with the Dark Horse to bring Barrion back.

We consider how to bring back the Dark Horse's sister. Marlee gets an emerald box to hold her phlogiston, but diGriz says that he destroyed her with the full Fire Sermon poem ritual.

Wedge goes back to the Paths of the Dead, finds the dead Richelieu and Speaks with Dead:

  1. How many male children did you have? Answer: 1
  2. Do you know his true name? Answer: Yes
  3. What was his true name? Answer: given; Wedge now knows it

Wedge returns and tells the name to diGriz, who summons by that true name (spending 2 Roke Points) as Marlee hooks him. Two creatures are drawn from the Abyss: Richelieu's Son and his nightmare Barrion. Each is at first level and completely helpless and disoriented. Maeglin takes charge of the invalids.

In response, the Dark Horse targets diGriz, but he makes his "save where there is no save" using his Roke clasp and with Threnody setting down a 20 on his personal die. Used against the Dark Horse, diGriz' clasp and Threnody's 20-sided personal die are lost. All end their adventure.
