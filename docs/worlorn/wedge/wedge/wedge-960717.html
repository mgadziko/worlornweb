Adventure record - July 17, 1996

Marlee begins on the asphault road on Spring Begins, year 4489 (year 45 on the Necrodemian calendar). By convenience, she defines that she was just outside Isuame. She joins Wedge in Isuame, currently called "Port Suame". Note: there is no dwarven castle in town. We bump into the current Salamander, Chup 2.

Wedge and Marlee sing for cash in a bar. diGriz joins (from Hidden Valley) via torch. He sees Chup conversing ("negotiating") with a female mage named Maja Barousse.

She's apparently Saltpeter's daughter, and Isuame is her place of power. [There appear to be two partially overlapping 16-mile radius places of power here.] "She owns this whole section of the country." diGriz converses with Chup. diGriz is looking for Peter Marlowe (the fire sword); Chup's grandfather (probably) died with it.

Outside the bar, Chup is attacked by Ironjade kav elfar (aided by Maja's probabilities). We notice and disrupt the attack. Marlee knows Maja's True Name. With it, she could take away the place of power.

Wedge is "seduced" by a group of wizard school co-eds (oh darn :-)

diGriz and Marlee converse with Maja Barousse. Maja asks for explicit notice should diGriz visit again. There is an intense standoff, until they agree leave. They grab Wedge.

We go to the Fountains of Paradise. Wedge teaches Paris High-Carsultyl Roland (High Priest) how to use the Fountains. diGriz and Maeglin discuss Peter Marlowe. The Fire Sermon is the only weilded elemental Sword, and Peter Marlowe does not currently exist.

For diGriz, Paris creates a perfect "fire rat investigation place". diGriz ponders the balance for several weeks (until 1-18): the Fire Sermon is an imbalance, if indeed it is alone. diGriz leaves when he discovers that Paris isn't actually in control of the Fountains. [It turns out that Threnody learned, via Paris via Wedge's training, how to make the Fountains his Turf!]
