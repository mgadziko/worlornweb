Adventure record - January 4, 1997

4-16-4494 (50 Necrodemus) Palace of New Illium in New Troad

Chrysofar, Wedge, Marlee...

-- SECRET INFORMATION --

Apparently someone shut down all dream knowledge of the occurrances during this adventure. Later information from witnesses from Watersea reveal that Chrysofar and Wedge came alone out of New Troad in the wind and were attacked by surprise by a sphere of annihilation - The End.

Presumably Marlee is still within New Troad, but she has not been seen. Since your scribe has no memory of these events, he would be extremely interested to find out whether she is OK and what, if anything, she knows of the events of this adventure!
