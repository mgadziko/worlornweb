Adventure record - December 4, 1996

Wedge (party leader), Marisse and Shayol begin at the Black Sapphire Inn, in Isuame on 3-12-4494 (50 Necrodemus).

We find a road priest Franklin Ironjade, and his teyn Larkin. We want to take the Road to Bird Keep. They say they'll take us on the road, if a truthsay corroborates my story of getting a guitar pick directly from Waldann.

We find a (copper samurai) bronzer and ask for the location of a truthsay. She says that Anthony is known to hang out at the Indigo (illusionist) wing of the wizard's school. Marisse asks for info on Bird Keep, but the bronzer apparently doesn't have any.

City encounters follow:

  1. We meet and pass some monks (they're lower level than Marisse).
  2. We're being tailed by a tall man with a Mandolin.
  3. Wedge gives a coin to a beggar, who warns "The minstrel is following you, and he is Death". Perhaps he is a former owner of the Harp; or perhaps he just recognizes it and wants it.
  4. We come across a crowd scene. A Hektorite faces down a thief until the bronzers come. Afterwards, the Hektorite "subtly" :-) tries to pick a fight with Wedge, but Wedge avoids it.
At the school, a man named Galt directs us to the Indigo wing. We find Anthony, who agrees to assist. We return to the Black Sapphire, and negotiate a price of 3000 gold; half to the truthsay, half to the road priest. (The inn charges 10% to make change from Wedge's gnome card.)

During the negotiation, Wedge verifies that he received the pick from Waldann's hand, that he is friends with the god of Life, and that he is the Messenger of the Gods (the kethi are suitably impressed :-) Marisse notices that another kav in the back is "giving signals" to Franklin (apparently a leader). Agreement is reached.

The kethi determine the distance to Bird Keep (it's three weeks). Wedge tries to memorize his own "Road travel" spell, but it fails due to his personal knowledge of Waldann's "fate". (He neglects to pass this information on the road priest.) He loans the pick, and gives a gold thumb to Franklin, who is successful in learning and memorizing the spell. It finally becomes clear that these Blood Law priests have not had road travel in many years. It is only by Wedge's actions that they have been able to regain it. In fact, he has to teach them the parameters and use of the spell, since these priests have never known them.

The party (Franklin, Larkin, Wedge, Marisse, Shayol, and two equestrian kethi carriage drivers) take a carriage on the road on 3-13-4494, and set destination for Bird Keep. The road does not affect Shayol, but as he is in the carriage we can take him with us. Encounters:

  1. Wedge - Vodalarians - a checkpoint. We show our passports and are allowed to pass.
  2. Marrise - A Songmaster - we pass by.
  3. Franklin - The Qualite - a great party of Lios Elfar. We avoid.
  4. Larkin - Steely Dan - carrying the tar baby. Wedge's legend lore: Champion ordered Steely Dan out on the road so he wouldn't have to hear the tar baby's loud racket. We avoid.
  5. Taal 1 - The Lokdal Dagger (Morganti weapon 058) - We all get headaches "for no good reason". [After the fact, it appears that the idiot Taal picked up the dagger.]
  6. Taal 2 - Brimstone - a big macho fighter. He avoids us. Our headaches continue.

We arrive at Bird Keep on 3-36-4494. We are refused entry. The kethi (and Marisse) get hot under the collar. Wedge uses the Harp to calm everyone, but melee ensues anyway.

The road priest only has enough spells memorized to take three (Shayol comes along "for free"). Marisse volunteers to stay behind (Wedge very reluctantly agrees), and we abandon the Taals. We set our destination one day's travel back up the road. Franklin (wisely) declines to draw an encounter. Fortunately, our headaches go away. Encounters:

  1. Larkin - Leo, but he's slumming - attacking Pellinese. We all avoid, except Shayol who can't. He kneels (when ordered) to Leo. They thank him by giving him 14 Leoland coppers.
  2. Wedge - Hal's Ankles - Faeries on Big Wheels. We avoid. They circle Shayol, but leave when he speaks Thari to them.

We arrive 3-37-4494 at 7am. Franklin and Larkin leave to return to Isuame when Wedge makes it clear he's returning to Bird Keep. This time we're taking wilderness encounters:

  1. 6pm - apparently an earth elemental. We run away! (We're faster so we leave it behind.)
  2. 9pm - wolves howling. It's a full moon, so Shayol can see acceptably. Apparently the earth elemental is still following, and the wolves attack it.
  3. 1am - three Shedu (winged lion divine servants) approach by surprise. They tell us that there's a Morganti ahead of us and a Morganti behind (could it be that pesky Minstrel?) They give us a ride the rest of the way to Bird Keep.

They won't touch ground in the 17 mile radius of Bird Keep, but let us off. Shayol falls off and damages himself quite severely. They quest Wedge to remove the morganti nature from the Bird Keep land law collection. We end our adventure in the wilderness outside Bird Keep in the early morning of 3-38-4494 (50 Necrodemus). This is not a safe place.
