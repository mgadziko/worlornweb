Adventure record - January 15, 1997

4-18-4494 (50 Necrodemus) Llewghund

Wedge begins; Threnody is already there. With Almitra, we begin planning a trip to Voidcastle -- without Chrysofar, our interest in Colors Layered has waned :-) The idea is to finally deal with the Crow, and Threnody is sure that we have to take it to a Pattern that is not in a Turf. He says he'll join us there, but not travel with us (perhaps he has some SECRET means of travel?) Apparently, he once had a discussion with the Dark Horse of a plan in which the Dark Horse would assist in taking Threnody's army to Voidcastle, if Threnody stood aside while the Dark Horse took back any former morganti victims there. At the time, Threnody knew of Maeglin, and wasn't willing to give him up, and so declined. Threnody guesses that there must be at least one other former morganti victim at Voidcastle as well.

Almitra provides the new mage Wedge with a beginning spellbook. She also changes her Midnight Mist into a voidskimmer, and with Threnody's help makes it an armored-5. She puts her turf focus, the Light of the World, into a bullseye lantern of Threnody's construction. Threnody also puts a Fire Shield on each of us -- Almitra, Wedge, Marisse and Shayol -- then leaves. Wedge suggests that Almitra have Yama make an Akashik record of her; she flips a coin and decides to live dangerously.

Almitra, Shayol, Wedge, Marisse and the Crow set out for Voidcastle in the Midnight Mist, with coordinates provided by Wedge. The journey takes nine days; the ship runs aground on 4-27-4494.

We disembark. It's very foggy. We rope ourselves together, and head straight out in the direction of the bow. We find the wall of the city, and to the right we find the gate already open. There are signs of a great battle: piles of dead humans, childeans, and kavalaars. Inside the fog is lessened, but there is the smoke of burning buildings. We head off toward the palace; the remnants of battle are all around.

As we come around a corner, we find ourselves in a fight between 4 Childeans and 16 Kavalaar (4 on the ground, 3 sets of 4 in the air on sky skoots). We join the fight on the Childean side, though one of the Childeans dies by the fire shield attacking us the first round.

In the battle, the Crow takes human form and becomes Simon, Almitra's grandson. Marisse and Simon attack in melee, Shayol begins a dance, Almitra begins a spell, and Wedge employs his death gaze. Magic missiles from the kavalaars interrupt Shayol and Almitra, and the kavalaars are apparently high on angel dust (which gives them a saving throw of 2) and so Wedge's attack is ineffective.

Marisse and Simon take out the kavs on the ground pretty quickly, but their friends' magic missiles nearly finish us off (Wedge's death shadow dies) before Simon grabs a nearby flask of water and uses his water power to Remove their boots. This makes it impossible for them to stay on their skyskoots, so they fall to their deaths. (Apparently, Simon has Llyr's demon amulet.)

Almitra uses her water heart to Remove all our damage; Simon shows her how to tune herself to it so it becomes a natural ability. During the battle, Almitra backed off and summoned a black doll, which might have brought her to the attention of the master of the place.

The Childean (the 3 still alive) tell us that they've been fighting house-to-house for (subjectively) roughly four weeks. We go with them to their "lair" and join the rest of the remaining Childean army. When they left to come here, Maeglin and Marlee were with them, but when the army arrived at Voidcastle Maeglin and Marlee were gone, so it's been a tough fight. Their opponents are a mix of human Dark Angels and Snake priests/crusaders, and Spider clan (Deep Coal Dwelling?) kavalaars, which was not what they were expecting -- they had thought that they would be facing Hellenes. The two sides are oddly balanced: the Childeans have magic resistance, while for the other side the Snake God recharges their wish rings and brings back the dead in its vats; the kavs are low level, but they are assisted by some high-level Dark Angels.

In addition, there is a large amount of spoils lying around all the dead bodies:

  • Childean dead -- galvorn chainmail, shields, crossbows, swords and maces; poison, decanters of endless water, manuals of iron golems, rings of multiple wishes, and staves of the magi
  • Kavalaar dead -- blades, chainmail, wands, staves, knives, sky skoots, helms of LEP, jade diadems, robes of powerlessness, angel dust, and blood daggers.
  • Snake Crusader dead -- victor's knives
  • Snake Priest dead -- rings of three wishes, staves of the adder, and python staves
  • Dark Angel dead -- rings of silent invisibility, daughters of albion, and angel blades

We dreamers suspend play, but don't end our adventure. Shayol ends, dancing. Only he may initiate play next in this place. It's 4-27-4494 (50 Necrodemus).
