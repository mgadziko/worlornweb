Adventure record - March 5, 1997

?-??-???? alt.Diaspar

Rib, alt.Wedge, Shayol, alt.Tanngrisner, alt.Dira begin with Shayol as party leader. Outside it is still winter and continual redlight. Wedge wishes, "I wish Marisse were alive and well with all her gear, standing just outside this city gate." Dira has to leave to fulfill the wish, but he appears the be unaffected by the redlight.

Rib takes party leadership, and takes Justin form to persuade Tann to make a second emerald box. Making two emerald boxes takes 100 days. During this time, Marisse appears outside. Tann invites her in, and she invites him out. She enters. She is a high-probability nightmare.

When it is complete, Rib enters one of the emerald boxes, which Shayol carries. Wedge carries the empty one. Wedge and Marisse exit. Shayol invites Wedge in, then he exits too. We walk to the nearby village of Thorp, where Shayol buys 8 weeks of food (Wedge and Marisse already have enough). Wedge slays a weaponmaker, to equip Marisse with a drow crossbow and bolts. We walk west to the coast, which takes three weeks. We reach the ruined city of windwolves. Surprising one, Wedge slays it. Just as we reach the beach, two more appear and are slain by Wedge.

Wedge summons the attention of Watersea. A boat comes to shore and we enter, just as a large company of windwolves approaches. Wedge gives the empty emerald box to the emissary. Some time later, redlight goes out. Later yet, the emissary brings the emerald box back full. Wedge trades the water heart for it. Wedge opens the emerald box that Shayol carries, and Rib emerges. Wedge gives her the full emerald box, which presumably carries the Light of the World.

Intending to return to the "original" universe, Rib wishes "I wish we were back where I was owner of the Llewghund Turf and Wedge was overlord of the Halfren Duchy." We find ourselves back at the ruins of Llewghund in winter. As Turf owner, she declares that no-one is hurt by redlight.

Shayol loots the Half-Elven One's decapitated corpse, taking his scroll tube and sash. Wedge takes his collapsible staff. Marisse begins searching for something. Shayol wishes "I wish Marisse knew where the item was." Dira appears just as Marisse finds something, slays her and takes it (the Rune of Secrecy?) He drops it; it was concealing a necklace. Dira wishes "I wish this sentient was incapable of bringing Tloluvin back into the main campaign." To fulfill this wish, Rib must surrender all name level Dust Law characters to the DM. Apparently, there is another sentient (T?) running around loose, looking like Marisse. Dira (re)takes the rune of secrecy, the necklace, and disappears.

Wedge wishes "I wish Marisse where alive and well with all her gear here and now." The real Marisse (not "alt.Marisse") appears. After some conversation, she realizes that this is not "her" Wedge. Marisse wishes "I wish we were back the minute before Wedge cast his wish in Voidcastle.", using a Lolth (galvorn) wish ring.

DISCONTINUITY
All is exactly as it was the minute before Wedge cast his wish in Voidcastle. Only our memories of the "alt.Worlorn" remain. Apparently, Rib is still Rib, and may ratify any levels/abilities gained during the alt.adventures. Wedge says, "I believe I won't make the wish I was just thinking about!"
