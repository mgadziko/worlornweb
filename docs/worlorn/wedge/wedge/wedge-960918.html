Adventure record - September 18, 1996

1-37-4489 (45 Necrodemus); diGriz, Shan, Almitra, Wedge and Marisse on Elidor at Morias.

Yama and Threnody arrive for an "off time" conversation with Wedge. As they enter, Yama is saying "...why don't you just let the bird kill you and get it over with?" (Is Marisse' crow out for vengeance for a wrongful death? Was Threnody involved/responsible?) Yama prefers the Stone Brothers be called "Jade Brothers", and says that they are no threat to him while they are under the Maker's control. They were concerned about Wedge's disapperance from Time last adventure. They leave as the adventure starts.

diGriz goes off by himself (to be away from Almitra). Saltpeter arrives and chews Almitra out for her actions last adventure and orders us off the island. Almitra says "A future Morgan told me it was a good thing." Saltpeter, pissed, responds "Begone!" He leaves, and Almitra is taken under robotic control (Saltpeter using the orb of sentient dragon control?) and becomes Marlee. diGriz returns, and Marlee (still controlled) roke removes the party to Carsultyl using the water heart. During transit, a copper remove 'asks' to remove the water heart from Marlee (the Prince?); she refuses, hanging on to it.

We appear on the beach at Carsultyl. Marlee arranges for korariel pins for Wedge, Marisse and diGriz. She (no longer controlled) goes off to find Maeglin. Wedge asks the date: it's 9pm on 1-30-4489, the evening of the day after Orbfest. He trumps Maeglin (who is still at High Asgar) to warn him of the impending tidal wave. Then he travels to the Paths to warn Yama of the upcoming pomerium. Yama is not surprised, and advises PCs to stay outside. Wedge travels to the Fountains to warn Threnody. He also is not surprised, and advises PCs to stay out. As this will seal off "troad", Threnody asks if Wedge wants anything from inside while there is still time. Wedge asks for, and receives, the Harp of Bragi. It has 24 strings, and legends tell that it can enrage or calm armies, break weapons, acts as a telesterion to Bragi, but has a "curse of truth". Wedge returns to Carsultyl.

Maeglin trumps Wedge to bring imperial people from High Asgar. The last through (Twaddlesham and Callion) bring a chest (carrying the High One's bird?). The chest is taken by Maeglin's people, and the imperials are closely, but unobtrusively, guarded by Childean soldiers. Wedge and Marlee calm the crowd.

diGriz uses a roke point to bind the hellflame to himself (a first step toward conquering hell). The dukes of hell are now aware of the binding.
