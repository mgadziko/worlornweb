Adventure record - September 25, 1996

1-31-4489 (45 Necrodemus) Llewghund

Almitra leaves Llewghund in her voidskimmer "Midnight Mist" to Watersea just outside Carsultyl. It is also a Ship of the Sea, and she confirms that Carsultyl has a Watersea port. However, her ship is blocked from entry by other ships. She dim doors to shore. She dim doors to the castle, and is taken to a holding cell by the Childeans.

Wedge, Marisse, diGriz and Shan are awakened and summomned by Maeglin. Twaddlesham died during the night by "natural causes". diGriz perceives that Twaddlesham "abandoned" his body. He has a large black ankh, which Wedge legend lores as a Talisman of the Sphere; diGriz takes possession. "Maeglin already has one; it's very nice!" Maeglin also apparently has a sphere of annihilation, and is not carrying a fire sword.

Maeglin says that Almitra is not welcome in Carsultyl, saying that he has "taken away Almitra's choice" of becoming Marlee. We go with Almitra to the beach. diGriz looks for his luggage (sapient pearwood, left behind on Elidor). It has arrived, but one of the two pieces is damaged -- apparently Saltpeter's "gift box" exploded; did the morganti escape or did the fire destroy it?

Marisse stays behind to do politicking. Wedge gives her his trump deck, and she gives him a strand of her hair so Almitra could summon her. Almitra tries to summon her water heart, but fails since Marlee gave it to Maeglin. Almitra, diGriz, Shan and Wedge board the ship at nightfall. Almitra allows diGriz to change the control console (by roke power) to best fit him. He sets destination for Farpoint in the Northlands; there are two options:

  1. "no time at all", but which would end the adventure, and
  2. "some encounters", and the adventure may continue.
He chooses "some encounters". There are no random encounters, and only one preset encounter: the Forbidden Isle. Who's going to touch ground? Almitra changes into a unicorn and steps out. Out of the jungle steps a man dressed all in red, 30-35, with long blond hair (very charismatic). He (Joiry) tells Almitra that he wants his light back, or barring that, he wants the hourglasses. Until he has what he wants, she will be in peril of redlight. He also tells her, "You're as much my mother as his" (meaning Simon). Almitra dim doors back to the ship.

The Forbidden Isle is a funnel point of travel by all methods except void, for which diGriz has not the training. A message bottle arrives from the Watersea for diGriz: 

    pomerium = Forbidden Isle
      |             |
    Troad       Northlands

Redlight falls in the middle of the day, then goes out (only Almitra is exposed). She goes below deck before it has a chance to recur. She restores the controls for herself, then takes us to the Fountains of Paradise.

She goes ashore, and is dazzled by the beauty. Threnody tells her, "I have no more power than you here. This is not my place." They discuss lighthouses, hourglasses, etc. diGriz pulls her back onto the boat.

diGriz again changes the controls for himself. There is no setting for New Nostoblet or Isuame, but there is for Saltpeter's Estate. There is no setting for Enseljos, but there is for the House Absolute. There is no setting for Musquel, but there is for Josie's Hut. He sets destination for Josie's, and we arrive at the small fishing village at 8am on 1-32-4489.

Wedge plays to influence reaction, and the villagers applaud. Josie greets diGriz warmly, and offers her traditional bread and water, which we all share. Josie is an older, blond woman, scarred by fire. Wedge plays "When Josie comes home", and she welcomes him.

There's a white picket fence separating "Musquel" from the rest of Lartroxia, recently erected. diGriz perceives that he, personally, could use his staff to enter Troad, by jumping over the fence. He tests it and is successful. He perceives that he is the first dreamer to enter. He perceives that the Muse of Dreams only awards ep in Troad, not in Wasteland. He could be awarded ep for jumping the fence, but he would lose it if he doesn't end his adventure in Troad. He also perceives that as "King Rat", he is platinum rank in Troad.

On the other side a small gate is visible. Normal conversation over the fence is possible. Shan trumps diGriz, fails. diGriz returns through the gate. 6 Wild Things (wrathmen) make sure that as he leaves nothing is allowed to enter.

diGriz to Josie: "Any messages to send?" She replies, "Say 'hi' to Damion." If diGriz can get Ko's phlogiston, Josie could use it.

We re-enter the boat. Almitra again resets the controls for herself and returns us to Llewghund. As Almitra enters, her adventure ends (but there is no perceptible change, as she is Turf owner). She sets a Turf Policy: redlight falls, but is harmless in Llewghund. She grants diGriz the language Mabrohoring, and the skill of piloting her voidskimmer (at least when set to "diGriz mode"). We clarify that her ship is an unarmored-5.

We end our adventure in Llewghund on 1-32-4489 (45 Necrodemus).
