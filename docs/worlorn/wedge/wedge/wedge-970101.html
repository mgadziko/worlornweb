Adventure record - January 1, 1997

4-12-4494 (50 Nec) midsummer in 3 days

Chrysofar, Almitra, Eressea have a turf discussion. The wielder of the muse of dreams (on isle of sleep; kin) warns about dream number conflicts for Chrysofar etc. Chrysofar asks about Watersea. Llyr responds that each ok. Chrys asks Llyr about psychotic Troy, Llyr says "ask Almitra". Llyr says the half-elven one used a "rune of secrecy", but Llyr was immune to the attack. Eressea asks if half-elven one attacked Tann; yes but he was also immune.

Threnody goes to Llewghund; Chrys and Almitra return to Llewghund. All go to find the half-elven one, but Almitra says he's not available (apparently he left Llewghund without her permission). Threnody advises Wedge to look for two things at Musquel, then leaves. Chrys ends in Llewghund.

Crosswinds definitions:

  • always night, with lots of stars and the northern lights
  • a rainbow bridge connects to the six accessible turfs
  • lots of hockey rinks
  • it's windy, but the perceived temperature is a matter of convenience

4-13-4494 (50 Nec) midsummer in 2 days

Marlee (party leader) & Maeglin, Chrys (10.3.1.7), Wedge (11.2.1,11.2.2,11.2.3) begin in Llewghund; Shayol joins from the Dry Land. Chrys arranges a word of recall "hedgehog" for Crosswinds.

We walk to the Dry Land and Yama joins; makes an akashic record of each of us. Yama will no longer use his mirror to Musquel (apparently he has been "punished" for accessing areas where Mark is DM.)

We return to Llewghund and go to the shore. Chrys asks Tann to come along; he demures. We use Almitra's mirror to view Musquel; it's a circular bay now surrounded by swamp. Yama returns to the Dry Land. Almitra sends the party through to Musquel (in a boat). local time: 7am 4-14

Chrys goes into the wind; connected to wasteland, Watersea, Crosswinds. She asks Llyr to temporarily withdraw the water from the bay. This leaves a 100-yard sphere centered at what used to be the village. Llewella arrives and speaks with the party: the Dark Horse likely had enmity with diGriz and Josie; probably Aleks, Josie, diGriz and any speakers at diGriz' flame were annihilated.

The water returns; we examine the white picket fence: there are two black rings on the fence. Marlee picks one up, gets the geas "give this to Wedge". Wedge takes it (it's the samurai rings) and gets a vision: an elf comes to the 100-yard diameter black sphere, takes off two rings and places them on the fence then leaps into the sphere (w/ a blue glow); voice "this may be all that's left of me; evermore they are yours to use as you wish" Wedge takes the other ring (the wizard ring) and gets another vision: Wedge can find the other pieces after he finds a successor. Wedge tells the party that the archmage of roke went into the sphere to try to fix the situation; these rings may give him something to come back to. Llewella asks who was the archmage. Wedge answers "the Star of Preservation". Maeglin examines the rings, then returns them.

Maeglin wants to visit Larteyn. We take our leave of Llewella. Using water and Chrysofar's wind, we _very_ quickly arrive at New Nostoblet, then Maeglin changes his mind. Chrys takes the party into the wind and then into Crosswinds; Wedge trumps Marisse and brings her through for a scenic tour of Crosswinds (we get souvenir T-shirts for Josephine and the two Solstices).

Shayol dances on an ice rink; he's successful despite a large penalty, and ends his adventure. The rest of the party returns to Llewghund via the rainbow bridge and ends at the duchy on 4-14-4494.

(Yama gets permission for his mirror to view and open in Crosswinds.)

4-15-4494 (50 Nec) midsummer

Chrys (party leader) begins in Llewghund (10.3.17, 11.2.1, 11.2.2, 11.2,3), with Shayol, Wedge, Marlee (9.2.25, 10.3.77, 10.7.24-misses roll-) and Maeglin. As Marlee begins, she faces a nightmare situation with Almitra. She uses her hook to retrieve Almitra from NPC dream 4.

We go to the castle. Marlee hands a note to the weapons master (Korishe) to take possession of the focus. At 7:02am, Chrys takes the party (Marlee, Maeglin, Shayol, Wedge) into the wind and heads at high speed for New Troad. In half an hour we arrive at Salt Peter's estate.

We come out of the wind in an alley in Isuame. Shayol begins his dance of preparation. We are 'accosted' by two bat samurai, until Chrys identifies herself as a bat ur-lord. They (Capt Laura and Lt Salicia) give us directions to the bat school, then send off the crowd.

Maeglin: "someone is concerned over my fire mastery". As we move toward the pomerium (Chrys can sense it in the wind) someone tries to shut down all winds; Chrys simply turns it back on. We pass the pomerium (Marlee notices that we're followed by bronzers).

Chrys tries Tann's trump; she sees herself as in a mirror. She takes the party back into the wind, then to the throne of New Troad. (again at high speed) -- apparently Chrys has a shard of the throne she can home in on.

The emerald dome is complete (finished 12 years ago), so we come out of the wind to enter. Chrys is _well_ received (by reputation). An honor guard escorts us to the palace. We're greeted by Thari-speaking lackeys and given refreshments. We're given quarters to rest and formal clothes then brought to a full formal court. Tann is dispensing court justice. After he's done, we're introduced to the court. Wedge introduces Shayol "the dancer". We're invited to sit on the dais near Tann. Afterwards the court breaks up into private "chats". Shayol 'cleverly' gets the rest of us away from Tann and Chrys who want a private chat.

Shayol, Wedge and Marlee perform for the court.

Tann takes Chrys to the Kolvir room. He apologizes for not coming to see her sooner to thank her for her actions on his behalf -- he's been bringing his court into order. He had hoped to share his realm with Chrys, but she has her own turf now.

Chrys has noticed some oddities about Tann. He confesses/claims that he is from the opposite side of a mirror of opposition; the victor over the original Tann on the 22nd intersection of the new 28 veil pattern. He says that the original Tann card should not be viewed outside New Troad as it will function as a mirror of opposition. He has made a new "Tann" card that responds to him. He says he needs a mirror of opposition in order to return to his side. Once there it should be safe to bring the original Tann back to life. This "Tann" is _not_ under Chrys' simulacrum control. He says he believes there are four mirrors at Voidcastle, but will need PC help to acquire one. Meanwhile he's been bringing order to _this_ New Troad as it will reflect back on _his_.

Not knowing that the real Tann didn't want it, this Tann brought the god Oberon into New Troad. The god is reflection, he is order.

We end in new troad (Shayol ends dancing) 4-15-4494.
