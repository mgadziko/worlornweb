Adventure record - December 11, 1996

Before the adventure, Wedge invokes the Compact (11.2.1, 11.2.2 and 11.2.3), "No morgantis on the adventure".

Wedge (party leader) and Shayol begin outside Bird Keep, on 3-38-4494 (50 Necrodemus) in the early morning. Marlee (with the Focus of Flambeau) appears in a burst of flame. We approach the keep and are challenged; "Who goes there?" Wedge trumps to Marisse, while Marlee responds by singing "Hello" [Beatles]. Marisse responds, and we all go through to her in the keep. [tearful reunion follows]

The crow is in an irridisium cage. We're introduced to Kayleigh (Duncan's mother and the current Madame Bird), who wants to usurp Maja Barrouse as Emporer. She discusses the possibility of appointing Marlee as Warlord. Kayleigh has a flesh sample from Simon in an emerald box. Marisse has received her second sword; now she simply needs training.

Wedge asks about the Morganti; Kayleigh replies that she did not allow it to affect her Place. Shayol is healed. Cure Light Wounds will affect him (as a Dancer), but not Cure Serious or Cure Critical. Shayol asks (in Thari) if there's a place to rest (he's had no sleep in two days); Kayleigh responds (in Thari), and arranges for quarters outside the keep. We all go rest.

Next day (3-39) we all (Wedge, Marisse, Shayol and Marlee) head for the nearby Copper Monastery, for Marisse' training. Encounters follow:

  1. Surprised by 6 spiders. Marlee comes out of surprise first; does open hand for a few rounds, then pulls out her gun with redsteel bullets. Wedge is appalled for a round. Marisse hits well with both weapons. 2855 Dust Law EP awarded. We search for and recover 4 bullets before
  2. a party of kav elfar "accidentally" wanders into our path. Marisse shows her copper weapons to signal them away (standing so they don't see the men), and they leave. 609 Dust Law EP awarded. Shayol finds a fifth bullet (still missing one).
  3. We come across a trap door in the ground. Two dozen orcs pour out. Wedge tries to parlay (in Pellinese) but they attack anyway. Shayol does well, until he is hit by a popsicle and is frozen. Then they break him into pieces. Marlee finishes hers off quickly, then shoots two of the three leaders standing at the back (the third escapes back into the trapdoor). Marisse lays into them effectively. As the battle turns against them, Wedge calls out in a Pellinese voice, "They're too tough for us; run away!", but the ploy doesn't work. Just before the end of the battle, he is hit by a popsicle too and is frozen.

    Marlee and Marisse recover 26 popsicles, 2 holy symbols and 2 small boxes (with control buttons). Marlee carries the pieces of Shayol, and Marisse carries Wedge. They head back for the keep. 4160 Dust Law EP awarded. Marlee takes party leadership.

  4. They encounter a bird-headed man in a drab green jumpsuit with a USAF patch. Marlee sings to him, and he chirps back. As they can't communicate, they part company. 230 Dust Law EP awarded.
  5. Arrive back at the keep at the same time as a trading party of Redsteel Kethi. They detect that Marlee is a shapeshifter and leave. No EP awarded.

Marisse and Marlee are healed back to full. Marlee wants to trump outside the Pomerium. (Kayleigh?) tells her that there is a white picket fence nearby at Twaddlesham. She and Marisse take Shayol and Wedge there (2 day's walk), and go through the gate (it's in a garden) and trump to (Threnody?) on 4-1-4494. Threnody puts Marisse in timestop.

Shayol is brought back to life. He has a dream that he drifted up, and a black thing took him to a place of paths. He was made to walk (not dance) on these paths, until Threnody came, took his hand and brought him back. Threnody tells him that from now on, his Dancer HTK "hits to kill" will act like hit points, so that cures will fully affect him. Wedge is "thawed" so that he doesn't die.

Thenody chastises Wedge for not reporting regularly; especially for not reporting that he'd seen the Half-Elven One. Apparently, the Half-Elven One has gone completely loopy, and set the Dark Horse onto Musquel, who summoned 7777 spheres of annihilation and turned the whole place into a bay. This also apparently destroyed all the Iakhlu and Er'Perhenne who were there, including Josie, Aleksandir and diGriz. Also, the Dark Horse has imminent plans for revenge upon Marlee and Wedge -- he apparently holds a grudge for his (unwilling) part in the restoration of Barrion and Richelieu's Son. Wedge says he's sorry, and he'll try to do better.

Wedge points out that there is an entry for him in the Compact, entitling him to the pieces of the Key that was Lost. Threnody looks up the section (12.1.36) and informs Wedge of the proper procedure to invoke it. He also suggests that Wedge stay with Marisse and the Crow, as it seems to have all five elemental powers, and even the Dark Horse may be afraid of it. He shows Wedge a vision of Marisse' part of the previous adventure after Wedge had left, in which the Crow uses Water to remove all the possessions of the combatents. Wedge points out that we left it at Bird Keep, but that Marisse does have an entry for it in the Compact. Threnody also suggests that Hannibal Lecter, as the creator(!) of the Dark Horse, may know its weaknesses.

Wedge takes back party leadership. We all (Wedge, Marisse, Shayol, Marlee) go to Llewghund. Marisse gets her training (1 week) from the Weaponmaster of Llewghund. We end at the Duchy on 4-9-4494 (50 Necrodemus).
