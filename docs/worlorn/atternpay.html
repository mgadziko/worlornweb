Hrenodytay, aboutyay atternspay eedingnay otay ebay ademay ithway ortalmay Roytay-loodbay? Efineday ortalmay Roytay loodbay....ikelay hetay odgay imselfhay ancay'tay oday ityay?

Exactlyyay: hetay odgay annotcay akemay ayay ewnay atternpay. Ityay asway Workinday'say lanpay hattay onay Odgay ouldcay avehay owerpay overyay hadowsay, osay ehay ademay ertaincay hattay ityay asway onlyyay Roytay loodbay, inyay ortalmay ormfay, hichway ouldcay ringbay orderyay romfay hetay omainsday ofyay Roytay.

Histay eansmay hattay Oberonyay, ifyay ehay tillsay existsyay, ouldcay evernay ringbay ackbay ishay ownyay Roadtay atternpay ithoutway ortalmay Roytay assistanceyay.

Hetay Atternpay ofyay Roadtay asway ademay ithway hetay loodbay ofyay Workinday (oneyay icknamenay orfay hetay repay-odgay ortalmay umanhay Roytay-howay-onceivedcay-Roadtay; Hrenodytay ashay _otslay_ ofyay amesnay orfay hetay ariousvay Roystay). Hentay odgay-Roytay (Oberonyay) atifiedray EVERYTHINGyay aboutyay Roadtay, includingyay hetay iabilitieslay: ikelay endyay-amegay onditionscay andyay ikelay hetay onay-odsgay-ancay-useyay-hetay-Atternpay Uleray, andyay ikelay indmay layersfay eingbay Inkay-anebay. Hatway ayay ollossalcay uckupfay hattay Oberonyay asway: atifyingray ityay allyay! Hatway ayay ozobay.
