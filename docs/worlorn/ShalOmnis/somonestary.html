Star Monestary of Shal Omnis

Location: Just outside of Imperial Enseljos
Purpose: Place of Knowledge and Training for StarMonks
Description: The Star Monestary of Shal Omnis is a simple building located on a the face of a cliff overlooking the ocean just outside of Imperial Enseljos. A lighthouse is manned by the monestary to aid ships in the night. The monestary houses a libary of many ancient works of knowledge and study. It is also a place where those humans who want to sacrifice their life to the study and discipline and workship of Shal Omnis might find solitude and furture their inner knowledge.
