Klawefen Statistics

Base Combat Statistics on the Klawefen (Speed: 6, 2 Handed Weapon)
Note: Because the Klawefen has no logical hand grips, special gloves called Busari (Boo-suh-reye) are worned by its wielders. The Klawefen is a very fast and powerful weapon, but requires a lifetime to fully master.

Blunt Damage S/M: 1-8, L: 2-8

Thrown Damage S/M: 1-8, L: 1-8
Slashing Damage S/M: 1-10, L: 3-18

Klawefen Bonuses

The Klawefen, when fully evolved, is a +7/+7 empathic weapon that increases to a +9/+9 intelligent weapon (LG) during starlight. The Klawefen is the holy symbol of Shal Omnis and establishes a symbiotic link with its wielder and the church.

For that reason, a wielder and Klawefen are bound for life. If a wielder would ever lose his or her Klawefen, then they would be without a weapon for the remainder of their lifetime. One wielder, one Klawefen. Lose your Klawefen and you have only two choices, regain the Klawefen or fight the rest of your life unarmed, without your holy symbol (followers of Shal Omnis are forbidden the use of any other weapon, less a dragonlance -- and don't think you will ever bond with a dragon without your Klawefen).

For the truly innocent and good person, they might consider a god-call to Shal Omnis for a replacement -- but be sure of your action, lest the ArchAngel rule against you and you find that it is you that has been replaced.

As the Klawefen evolves with its owner, its power as a weapon increases and its embedded spell effects begin to emerge.  Spell effects are single charges that recharge automatically when the Klawefen is exposed to starlight. The spell effects will not recharge until the weapon is exposed to starlight.
