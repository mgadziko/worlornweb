Tai-Shal-Li (The Way of the Dragon)

 Tai-Shal-Li is an ancient fighting style that combines the versalitity and finese of martial arts with the grace and power of the dragon. A lost art, rumored to have been first taught to mortals by a dragon named Kymarcnar, this fighting style incorporates both an armed and unarmed techniques. The StarKnight school teaches the armed variation (Tai-Shal-Wu) using the Klawefen . The StarMonk school teaches the unarmed discipline (Tai-Shal-Ki) to those non-warrior monk/thief* followers of Shal Omnis.

Training is rigorous and as such, all non-weapon proficiencies must be allocated to this skill, until the 15th level is achieved. Specific benefits of the discipline for each dicipline are listed below.
