The Star Chamber



Star Light, Star Bright,
First Star I See Tonight,
I Wish I May, I Wish I Might,
Have the Wish, I Wish Tonight.

The Star Chamber is one of the most incredible feats of technology and  magic ever assembled. The Star Chamber is a vehicle that is capable of  traveling to any location, but it can often be found floating on the Astral Plane above Mestea. The Star Chamber is both a Sanctuary, a Place of Power, and a Fortress, capable of harnessing the awesome power of the stars (cold-fusion being only one example).

From the Star Chamber, adventurers could travel to any place, perhaps any time. The Star Chamber is the key for adventures who want to travel  places they could not otherwise go. Wish upon a star to Shal Omnis and her servant Omnis GoodKnight may be sent to help thee.

The Star Chamber is essentially Mobile Turf. Conceivably, The Star Chamber can travel to and from turfs and domains, effectively creating the existence of an inter-turf turf. The Star Chamber is an impenetrable fortress, capable of interstellar,  inter-planar, inter-turf, multidimensional travel and defended by the ultimate harnessing of the power of the stars. In fact, the Rune Stones are stars and be found within this place.

Omnis GoodKnight can travel to and from the Star Chamber at his will, from any  location. Omnis GoodKnight cannot die permanently due to the power of the Star Chamber. At  the moment of death, he and his equipment are instantaneously returned to the Star Chamber where they are restored fully to perfect condition. No one can claim Omnis GoodKnight's spirit, for Shal-Omnis has already done so and the Alter of Shal-Omnis is the focal point of this power.

The alter also has the following powers:Resurrection, Regeneration,  Healing, Restoration, Stasis Field (no aging while laying one it), Turns to Dust any non-believer/person who has fallen from grace, Wall of Force  10' around alter when believer is on it.

From the Star Chamber, Omnis GoodKnight has a personal gateway to Shal-Omnis. From the Throne of the Star Chamber, Omnis GoodKnight can pilot the Star Chamber and  harness its awesome power. The Eyes of Shal-Omnis allow Omnis GoodKnight to scry  undetected on anyone or any location, allowing an ESP-like link to the subject being  viewed, and allowing an Augury to answer any questions about what is being  viewed. This scrying device also serves as the view screen of the Star Chamber.

There is a matter-transporter (much like Star Trek) that Omnis GoodKnight can use to transport others to and from the Star Chamber. For others, this is the only way to and from the star chamber.  The transporter can be controlled only by Omnis GoodKnight  while setting on the throne.

Omnis GoodKnight also commands the power of the TRUE PLATINUM WISH GLOBE which allows him to cast one wish per day without penalty. The Globe is  empathic to a degree to assist in determining true desire. The Globe is  made of true platinum (the rarest of metals).

One of the guardians of the Star Chamber is a Black Worg named Blade or just Dog. The Worg was obtained during a battle/test with three "FATES.'  This Worg is a "Reflection of NiteBlade", a figure who can be found in the historical records of Worlorn.  The Alignment and General  attitude of the worg are exactly those of that were NiteBlade's before his transformation.. The animal is as a familiar to Omnis GoodKnight except no  HPs are received by Omnis. The animal will be loyal until death. The Worg has an empathic link to Omnis and will act as a trained attack or guard animal would.  The worg has a MAGIC RESISTANCE of 25%.

The armory and treasury of the Star Chamber is vast, containing many  various and unique weapons, works of art, treasures, and raw materials.  The library of the Star Chamber contains lost and rare works of art, literature, and  magic. One would have trouble finding a better library anywhere.

All items found with the Star Chamber belong to the Church of  Shal-Omnis, and thus, don't overtly violate any potential restrictions Omnis GoodKnight may or may not have placed upon him. In fact, you would be hard pressed to find someone who has given more of himself to a deity or church.. All items within the Star Chamber are available to the followers of Shal Omnis and may be granted by Shal Omnis to them. 



Omnis GoodKnight
The Archangel and Son of  Shal Omnis




Omnis GoodKnight is the Dominator and is Supreme within the Star Chamber. This is his solitude  · his sanctuary. This is his fortress; his gate to Shal-Omnis.

To humanoids, Omnis GoodKnight may appear either as a humanoid male or as a majestic dragon. The humanoid form displays long flowing hair the glows in the star light and whose eyes penetrate the deepest of souls.  Clothed holy STAR ARMOR and with the with a Klawefen strapped across his back, Omnis GoodKnight projects the glory and love of his soverign Shal Omnis.  

In dragon form, Omnis GoodKnight appears as a majestic dragon of light whose wings extend to the horizons. His glowing eyes convey the love and will of Shal Omnis to her people. Omnis GoodKnight is the Archangel Supreme for Shal-Omnis, the supreme Deity of Dragons. 

Omnis GoodKnight is GOOD, in its purest since.
 



Stars
omnipresent  archetype


Stars have been described as the gateways for those who might wishing upon a star; the empowerers of domains; the sun; helpful guides to travellers at night; pinholes of light in the sky up in that big black thing; big, swirling balls of gas; tellers of astrology and fortunes; the place with the kings of the past look down upon us; angels; lights from heaven; and the very sustainer of life itself.  Whatever their purpose or their power, the serve to guide mortals and immortals alike, always shining and bringing hope to an otherwise dark land.
