Class Benefits to Followers of Shal Omnis

Holy Symbol of Shal Omnis
All classes are accepted and welcome within the loving arms of Shal Omnis.  For specific classes, additional benefits are given, as described below.  Specific training for Paladins and Clerics occur in the city of Imperial Enseljos at the following places: The Star Temple of Shal Omnis, and The Star Paladin School of Shal Omnis.

Cleric (StarBringer)  Paladin (StarKnight)     Ranger (MoonKnight)
Mage (StarComet)   Illusionist (StarGalaxy)  Warrior (NovaKnight)
Thief (DarkStar)      StarMonk* (Nebulae)

Cleric (StarBringers) / Paladin (StarKnights)

The sole weapon of the holy followers of Shal Omnis is the Holy Symbol of Shal Omnis ... known as  the Klawefen (Claw-eh-Fenn), or Claw of the Dragon.  This fierce and ancient weapon is designed to function as a blunt, piercing, slashing, or even throwing weapon by application of various grips in the hands by a trained StarBringer/StarKnight. 

Clerics and Paladins are specifically trained at their respective schools in this lost weapon (Klawefen) and its respective fighting style called Tai-Shal-Li (Tie-Shal-Lee).  Clerics and Paladins are required to put all weapon proficiency slots into Klawefen and all non-weapon proficiency slots into Tai-Shal-Li until they reach the 15th circle of enlightenment (15th Level).

StarBringers and StarKnights wear the armor of the galaxy called StarLight armor.

StarBringers and StarKnights must be Lawful Good.

StarMonk (Special)

The StarMonk is a special class available only to humans.  To become a star monk, one must first train as a StarBringer to 3rd level.  Then train as a StarComet to 3rd level. Afterwards, train as a DarkStar to 3rd Level.  Once the person has completed the basic training, they become a 1st Level StarMonk and are eligible to begin training in Tai-Shal-Ki as an unarmed fighting discipline. Starmonk's are forbidden from using weapons.

StarMonk's follow the Thief experience table (2nd level StarMonk=4th Level Thief).  StarMonk's may use all the skills learned in their training.

StarMonk's must be Lawful Good.

MoonKnight (Ranger)

MoonKnight's are an ancient order of Knights that have been loyal to Shal Omnis since the first mortal contact. MoonKnights receive the following items from the church:

StarSextent (100% guaranteed navigation when stars are present)
StarPathfinder (20% increase in tracking when stars are present)

MoonKnights must be Good.

DarkStar (Thief)

Darkstars are not thieves in the typical sense, but rather, highly trained scouts. Darkstars receive a Darkstar cloak that bends light to render the wearer 90% invisible. 

Darkstars receive a Sanctuary ring that cast sanctuary on command twice a day. Darkstars must be good.

NovaKnight (Warrior)

NovaKnight's are warriors.  They receive not special skills from the church. After 7th level, NovaKnights in good standing may request a StarSword from the church. The StarSword functions as a Sword of Sharpness.

NovaKnights are encouraged to be good.

StarGalaxy (Illusionist)

StarGalaxy's are illusionist that serve the church. StarGalaxy's automatically have Astrology as a skill. StarGalaxy's are well-known for using the stars to foretell the future. As such, their Astrology skill increases twice as fast as normal for every non-weapon proficient point spent in training.

StarGalaxy Illusionists must be Chaotic Good.

StarComet (Mage)

StarComet are mages that specialize in the research of space and stars. As such, StarComet mages are guaranteed to learn all spells related to space and stars.  In addition, where applicable, damage will be double when casting such spells.  For example, if a StarComet mage casts Meteor Shower, the resulting damage would be twice that of normal damage.

StarGalaxy Mages must be Neutral Good.
